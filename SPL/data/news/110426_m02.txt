**🏏 SPL S07, Match 2: LSG vs PBKS – N Rana Steers a Thrilling Chase**

A tense, see-saw battle saw PBKS chase down 184 with just two balls to spare. LSG, opting to bat first, lost Shubman Gill early, but FH Allen (66 off 37) and WG Jacks (41 off 36) steadied the ship. However, MP Yadav’s fiery 3/45 and Shashank Singh’s clever 2/34 kept LSG to 183/8 — a challenging but gettable total.

In reply, PBKS lost Donald early, but V Suryavanshi (72 off 48) and N Rana (74* off 51) added a match-winning 124-run stand. LSG struck back with two quick wickets, but Rana held his nerve. With 14 needed off the final over, Vansh Bedi (15* off 4) smashed a four and a six to seal a dramatic win. Rana’s calm, unbeaten knock earned him Player of the Match.
