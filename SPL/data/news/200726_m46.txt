# 🏏 Match 46 – GT vs DC: A Run-Fest for the Ages! 💥

DC won the toss and chose to bowl first on a pitch that promised to be a paradise for batters. GT’s openers started cautiously but quickly found their rhythm. The momentum shifted when DS Airee struck twice early, but a resilient 65 from SV Samson and a blazing unbeaten 84 from D Brevis propelled GT to a massive total of 245.

Chasing a daunting target, DC lost their opener early, but then came Tarang Gohel. The man was on a rampage, smashing a breathtaking century that dismantled the GT bowling attack. His 117-run masterclass, perfectly complemented by N Sindhu’s 63, turned the chase into a one-sided affair. Despite some fight from GT’s bowlers, DC chased down the total with 6 wickets in hand, completing a comfortable victory. It was a night where the bat completely dominated the ball.
