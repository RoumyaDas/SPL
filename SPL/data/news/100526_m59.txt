# 🏏 SPL S07, Match 59 – KKR vs DC: The Shedge Massacre & Kishan's Lone War

KKR won the toss and batted first on a pitch that loved pace 3.2x more than spin. Then the innings almost fell apart.

Fraser-McGurk fell cheaply (3), Pepper crawled to 39 off 35, and at 88/2 after 12 overs, KKR were staring at a below-par total. Then Ishan Kishan decided otherwise. He unleashed a masterclass — **105 off 59 balls** with 11 fours and 4 sixes — holding the innings together like a man possessed. Bumrah's 3/40 kept DC in the game, but Kishan's brilliance dragged KKR to **180/5**.

Then came the insanity.

DC's chase lasted 13.4 overs. And they were never in it.

Suryansh Shedge — yes, the same guy who bowled tidy spells — produced **5/19** in 4 overs of absolute witchcraft. Maxwell (first ball duck), McMullen (13), Dalal (7), Sindhu (1), Lawrence (0), Gohel (11), Sunny Sandhu (15)... Shedge ate them for breakfast. B Kumar (2/23), Pulkit Narang (1/9), and Ramandeep (1/9) joined the party.

DC collapsed like a house of cards: **15/4** inside 4 overs, **35/6** at the end of Powerplay, and finally **92 all out** in 13.4 overs.

KKR won by 88 runs. A demolition.

**Winner:** KKR by 88 runs  
**Player of the Match:** Suryansh Shedge (KKR) – for a stunning 5-wicket haul
