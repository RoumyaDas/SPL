# 🏏 SPL S07, Match 54: CSK vs MI – A Spin Web and a Clinical Chase

The pitch at CSK was a spinner's paradise, offering 3.3x more assistance to spin than pace. MI won the toss and elected to bowl first, a decision that proved inspired as their spinners choked the life out of CSK's innings.

CSK's batting never really found fluency. **Tilak Varma (59 off 39)** held one end together, but wickets fell regularly around him. **AJ Hosein (3/32)** was the pick of the bowlers, extracting sharp turn and bounce. Late blows from **Karun Nair (32)** and **Tanush Kotian (24)** pushed CSK past 200, but they always looked short on a tricky surface.

Chasing 201, MI made a mockery of the target. **Urvil Patel (94* off 50)** played a stunning knock, mixing caution with aggression. He found an able partner in **Mitchell Marsh (46* off 23)** , and together they steered MI home with 16 balls to spare. CSK's spinners found no purchase as Patel swept and drove them to all parts. A clinical chase that never broke a sweat.

**Result:** MI wins by 8 wickets (17.2 overs)  
**Player of the Match:** Urvil Patel (MI)
