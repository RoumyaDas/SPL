📋 **Match 32 – GT vs KKR (Venue: GT)**

GT won the toss and put KKR in to bat on a spin-friendly pitch. What followed was absolute carnage. Jake Fraser-McGurk set the tone with a breathtaking 67 off just 23 balls, smashing 10 fours and 3 sixes. Rohit Sharma then played the finisher's role to perfection — an unbeaten 52 off 21 balls with 6 sixes. KKR lost wickets regularly but kept the accelerator pressed, posting a mammoth 226/9. George Linde was the pick of GT bowlers with 3 wickets, but he went for runs like everyone else.

The chase started explosively — Samson and Inglis added 95 in no time, both scoring quick fifties. After their dismissals, Naman Dhir stood tall, playing a captain's knock of 59* off 34 balls. GT needed 16 off the last over but could only manage 9, finishing at 220/4 — falling just 7 runs short in a run-fest. KKR's bowlers held their nerve in a game where 446 runs were scored in 40 overs. Absolute mayhem. 🔥💥

**Player of the Match:** J Fraser-McGurk (KKR)
