📝 **Match Summary – SPL S07, Match 6: RCB vs GT**

Toss: RCB won the toss and elected to bowl first, expecting early help for their pacers.

GT’s innings never quite caught fire. **JP Inglis** (45 off 28) looked fluent, but wickets kept tumbling at the other end. Samson fell to a sharp run-out, and the middle order struggled to break free. **V Nigam** (31 off 17) provided a late spark, but **SH Johnson** (3/36) kept chipping away, ensuring GT never got away. A below-par 184 on a pace-friendly pitch.

Chasing 185, RCB’s openers turned the chase into a demolition. **CH Gayle** (104* off 51) was at his brutal best – 12 fours, 4 sixes, striking at nearly 204. **Atharva Taide** (77* off 52) played the perfect anchor role, rotating strike and punishing the bad balls. Together, they stitched an unbroken 185-run stand, finishing the game with 2.5 overs to spare. GT’s bowlers had no answers – no wickets, no pressure, just pain.

RCB cruised home by 10 wickets in a complete mismatch.
