🏏 **SPL S07 Match 37: SRH vs KKR – Openers Gun Down 174 in Style**

KKR opted to bat first on a balanced pitch but lost Rohan Kunnummal first ball. **Jake Fraser-McGurk** (51 off 22) launched a sensational counterattack, while **Ishan Kishan** (65* off 48) anchored the innings. KKR finished at 173/7 – a competitive but chasable total. **Nathan Ellis** (3/41) and **Adam Zampa** (2/23) were the pick of SRH bowlers.

In response, SRH openers **P Simran Singh** (74 off 39) and **Sahil Parakh** (38 off 22) put on a rapid 89-run stand. After Parakh fell, **Jos Buttler** (43* off 25) and **Kartik Sharma** (23* off 10) finished the chase with absolute ease. SRH raced to 179/2 in just 16 overs, winning by 8 wickets with 4 overs to spare. KKR's bowlers had no answers – Starc and Wiese went for 15 and 13.67 runs per over respectively.
