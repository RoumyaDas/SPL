# 🏏 SPL S08 - Match 23: GT vs KKR

## A Spin Web and a Green Masterclass! 🕸️

On a surface tailor-made for spinners, KKR's bowlers spun a web around GT's batting lineup. Despite a quickfire cameo from **JP Inglis** (22 off 7) and a resilient 32 from **V Nigam**, GT kept losing wickets at regular intervals. **Parvej Musaraf** (3/37) led the charge, ably supported by **DR Briggs** and **Abrar Ahmed** who choked the run flow, restricting GT to a below-par 159.

Chasing 160, KKR had a shaky start losing early wickets, but **C Green** (64* off 47) anchored the innings with a mature knock. He found an able partner in **WL Madsen** (28 off 22) to steady the ship, and later **Jay Gohil** (21 off 15) provided the late impetus. Despite a few hiccups, Green held his nerve and guided KKR home with 9 balls to spare, showcasing his all-round brilliance. 💪
