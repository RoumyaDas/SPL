**Match 29 – GT vs SRH**: A Spin Masterclass and a Nerve-Shredding Chase 🏟️

On a track tailor-made for spinners, GT's decision to bowl first paid off in spectacular fashion. Sufiyan Muqeem was simply unplayable, weaving a web that snared five SRH batsmen, while Ashutosh Sharma chipped in with three crucial wickets. Despite a breathtaking 72 off just 29 balls from Kartik Sharma, SRH were bowled out for 186, setting up what seemed like a straightforward chase.

But cricket is never that simple. GT's chase began confidently but soon turned into a tense affair as wickets tumbled at regular intervals. At 153/5 with just over three overs left, the game hung in the balance. Enter the unheralded GF Linde, who smashed a quickfire 19 off 7 balls, and the resilient V Nigam, who anchored the innings with a composed 44*. Together, they steered GT home with 4 balls to spare, completing a thrilling 3-wicket victory. It was a testament to GT's depth that they could overcome a mid-innings collapse to seal the win.
