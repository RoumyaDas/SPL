**Match 27 – PBKS vs LSG**: A Nail-Biting Finish at the PBKS Fortress 🏟️

The pacers had their say on a track built for speed, and what a contest it turned out to be. LSG, opting to bat first, rode on a magnificent 66* off 40 from Abishek Porel and a solid 52 from Ajay Rohera to post a daunting 195. JJ Smit was the lone warrior with the ball, picking up 3 crucial wickets, but the others bled runs freely.

The chase was a high-octane thriller. PBKS lost early wickets in a heap, slipping to 24/3, but a magnificent 73 off 46 from PHKD Mendis and a fighting 55 from TL Seifert kept them in the hunt. They brought the equation down to the final over with 14 needed, but Arshdeep Singh, the Player of the Match, held his nerve brilliantly. He picked up 3 wickets in the final over, including two in three balls, to seal a heart-stopping 7-run victory for LSG. It was a classic case of momentum swinging wildly until the very last ball.
