📝 **SPL S07 Match 24: LSG vs CSK – Spin Web Snares CSK as LSG Cruise**

On a pitch tailor-made for turn, CSK opted to bat first – and immediately found themselves in a maze. Wickets kept tumbling as LSG’s spinners operated in a ruthless convoy. H Ssenyondo (3/32) and Arshdeep Singh (3/50) led the demolition, but the real dagger came from Kuldeep Yadav (2/28) and Shubhang Hegde (2/23), who never let CSK breathe. Only Heinrich Klaasen stood tall, smashing a breathtaking 67 off 34 balls with 5 sixes, dragging CSK to a shaky 173 all out. No one else crossed 33.

The chase was a formality. Shubman Gill set the tone with a fiery 31 off 13, and DR Mousley (48 off 35) anchored calmly. But it was Will Jacks who finished in style, walloping an unbeaten 38 off just 15 balls. LSG strolled to 175/5 in 17.1 overs, winning with 17 balls to spare. The spinners had won the game inside the first innings – the chase was just a victory lap. 🏏🕸️
