📝 **SPL S07 Match 23: PBKS vs MI – A Low-Scoring Thriller That Went Down to the Wire**

On a pitch offering generous help to pacers, MI chose to bat first – and their openers came out swinging. Abhishek Sharma blazed 30 off just 13 balls, while Urvil Patel anchored with a steady 63 off 43. But PBKS fought back through JJ Smit, who delivered a miserly 3/24, and kept chipping away at wickets. MI never really broke free, finishing at a modest 188/6 – a total that felt at least 15 runs short.

Chasing, PBKS lost N Rana early, but V Suryavanshi (51 off 30) kept the chase alive. However, wickets fell at regular intervals, and the required rate kept climbing. Enter Shashank Singh, who smashed an unbeaten 46 off 25, and Vansh Bedi, who contributed a fighting 39*. With 19 needed off the final over, Ravi Teja held his nerve, conceding just 9 runs. PBKS fell agonizingly short by 9 runs.

A tense, low-scoring classic where every run mattered. MI held their nerve to defend a below-par total. 🏏💥
