**🔥 SPL Season 6 – Eliminator: Kolkata Knight Riders vs Lucknow Super Giants – Match Summary**

In a high-voltage Eliminator played on a surface that offered massive assistance to spinners (2.3x), Lucknow Super Giants pulled off a heist to eliminate KKR and march into the next round.

Batting first, KKR looked in complete control. Sunil Narine (43 off 23) and Jake Fraser-McGurk (28 off 22) gave them a flying start, and Cameron Green (50 off 33) continued the momentum. But just as KKR looked set for a massive total, left-arm spinner Henry Ssenyondo turned the game on its head. He removed Narine, Fraser-McGurk, and later struck twice in the death to finish with a sensational 4/32, restricting KKR to 198/5.

In response, LSG openers Shubman Gill (53 off 26) and Ryan Rickelton (38 off 20) launched a brutal assault, racing to 76/0 in 6 overs. Rickelton fell soon after, and Gill followed for 53, leaving LSG at 94/2. But Will Jacks (61* off 38) held his nerve, finding an able partner in Jos Buttler (39* off 25). The duo added an unbeaten 84 off just 49 balls, with Jacks finishing the game in style with a six. LSG chased down 199 with 5 balls to spare, knocking KKR out in a thrilling Eliminator.
