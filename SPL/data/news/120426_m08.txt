📝 **Match Summary – SPL S07, Match 8: LSG vs KKR**

Toss: LSG opted to bat first on a spin-friendly pitch (2.1x spin assistance).

LSG’s innings never found rhythm. Shubman Gill (24 off 8) smashed three sixes in a cameo, but wickets fell in a procession. **DR Mousley** (63 off 45) held one end together with a gritty half-century, but no one stayed with him. KKR’s spinners **S Lamichhane** (3/22) and **Pulkit Narang** (2/20) strangled the middle order. LSG limped to 153/8 – well below par.

Chasing 154, KKR lost Ishan Kishan early to a run-out. **BM Duckett** (34 off 18) and C Green (31) kept hopes alive, but the moment spin came on, the chase unravelled. **H Ssenyondo** (2/10 from 3 overs) and **Arshdeep Singh** (3/26) triggered a collapse – 5 wickets fell for 12 runs. From 109/6 to 124 all out. Lamichhane’s late cameo (16) was too little.

LSG defended a low total by 29 runs – a spin choke on a turning track.
