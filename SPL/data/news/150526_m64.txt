🚀 **SPL S07, Match 64: KKR vs RCB – A Five-Star Special from Bhuvi**

RCB opted to bat first on a pace-friendly track at KKR. **CH Gayle** (37 off 16) and **Salil Arora** (45 off 21) provided fireworks, but the innings was held together by **JM Sharma** (48* off 29). However, the spotlight belonged to **B Kumar**, who ran through the lower order with a stunning **5-wicket haul**, his figures reading 5/21 in 4.3 overs. RCB were bowled out for **214** in 19.5 overs.

Chasing 215, KKR came out swinging — **J Fraser-McGurk** (25 off 13) and **Ishan Kishan** (30 off 15) kept the rate healthy. But **SS Ghosh** (4/20) and **HJ Manenti** (3/31) triggered a spectacular collapse. KKR lost their last 7 wickets for just 44 runs, crumbling to **147 all out** in 16 overs. A brutal 68-run demolition — RCB win by 68 runs.
