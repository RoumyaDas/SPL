**🏏 Match 66: KKR vs CSK – Summary**

In a thrilling contest at Eden Gardens, CSK posted a commanding 190/7 after being asked to bat first. Tilak Varma starred with a blistering 49 off just 22 balls, while Pollard chipped in with a vital 28. Bhuvneshwar Kumar was the pick of the KKR bowlers with a fine 3/28.

KKR's chase started explosively with Fraser-McGurk (27 off 12) and Narine (31), but they kept losing wickets at crucial junctures. Ravi Singh (35) and AGS Gous (34) fought back, but CSK's unlikely hero with the ball was Kieron Pollard, who delivered a stunning spell of 2/9 in 2 overs to flip the game. Despite a late six from Jurel, KKR fell short by 8 runs.

CSK snatch a dramatic win, and Pollard's golden arm earns him the Player of the Match.
