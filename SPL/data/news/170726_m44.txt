# 🏏 SPL S08 - Match 44: RR vs MI

**Toss:** MI won the toss and chose to bowl first on a balanced pitch.

The first innings witnessed absolute carnage from the RR opener **Aniket Verma**, who played one of the most destructive knocks of the season. His 110 off just 49 balls was a masterclass in power-hitting, studded with 16 boundaries and 3 sixes. He found initial support from **S Dube** and later from **Kumar Kushagra**, who played a crucial cameo. However, it was the late assault from **YBK Jaiswal** that truly broke MI's spirit, as he smashed 34 off just 9 balls to propel RR to an imposing total of 241.

In response, MI's chase began with breathtaking intent as openers **Tushar Raheja** and **Urvil Patel** went hammer and tongs. After their dismissals, **Abhishek Sharma** anchored the innings with a classy 81, keeping MI in the hunt. He found support from **MR Marsh** and **Siddhesh Veer**, who played some delightful strokes. However, the required rate kept climbing, and despite a late flourish, MI fell short in their pursuit. RR's bowlers held their nerve in the death overs, with **Karan Lal** and **KA Pollard** being particularly impressive.

RR wins by 21 runs, defending a mammoth total!
