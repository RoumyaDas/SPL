# 🏏 MATCH SUMMARY: SRH vs GT | SPL Season 4 | Match 44

**Venue:** SRH Home Ground  
**Toss:** SRH won the toss and elected to bat  
**Result:** SRH won by 26 runs

---

## 🔥 KEY MOMENTS & TURNING POINTS

### **1st Innings: SRH – 234/4 (20 overs)**

- **Explosive Start!** Openers **Priyansh Arya** (29 off 17) and **P Simran Singh** (81 off 45) launched SRH to 58/0 in the PowerPlay. Simran was particularly brutal, smashing 7 sixes!
- **Rahul’s Cameo:** **KL Rahul** (47 off 27) continued the assault with a fiery knock, including 4 fours and 2 sixes.
- **Reddy’s Fireworks:** **Nithish Kumar Reddy** (42* off 15) and **PHKD Mendis** (16* off 7) demolished the death overs, adding 69 runs in the last 5 overs!
- **GT Bowling Struggles:** Except for **Swapnil Singh** (2/65), every bowler was taken to the cleaners. Rashid Khan went for 50 runs in his 4 overs.

### **2nd Innings: GT – 208/6 (20 overs)**

- **Samson’s Blitz:** **SV Samson** (48 off 21) started like a house on fire, hitting 4 fours and 3 sixes. GT raced to 54/0 in 5 overs.
- **Middle-Order Collapse:** After Samson’s dismissal, GT lost **Pepper** (0) and **Inglis** (7) cheaply. **KA Maharaj** (2/41) and **Mohammad Ghazanfar** (1/52) applied the brakes.
- **Gaikwad’s Lone Fight:** **Ruturaj Gaikwad** played a magnificent lone hand, scoring **97* off 55** with 6 fours and 5 sixes. But he ran out of partners.
- **Mendis’ Crucial Strikes:** **PHKD Mendis** (2/42) removed set batters at key moments, ensuring SRH stayed ahead.
- **Too Much to Do:** Despite Gaikwad’s heroics, GT fell 27 runs short in a steep chase.
