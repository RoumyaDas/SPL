# 🏏 SPL S08 – Match 18: PBKS vs GT

In a spin-dominated contest where the ball turned sharply, GT produced a clinical all-round performance to dismantle PBKS and secure a comprehensive victory.

GT, batting first, recovered from an early wobble to post a commanding 197/4. Naman Dhir anchored the innings with a fluent 63, while JP Inglis and D Brevis chipped in with vital cameos of 46 and 46* respectively — Brevis unleashing a brutal assault in the death overs.

Chasing 198, PBKS started positively with a 55-run opening stand, but the introduction of spin changed the game entirely. Sufiyan Muqeem and V Nigam spun a web around the PBKS batters, claiming 3 and 2 wickets respectively. PBKS crumbled under pressure, losing wickets in clusters, and were eventually bowled out for just 151 in 16.5 overs — falling short by 46 runs in a one-sided affair.
