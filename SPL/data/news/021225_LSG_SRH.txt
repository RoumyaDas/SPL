Match 75, SPL S05 : LSG v SRH

Match 75 saw LSG host SRH after SRH won the toss and elected to bowl. LSG's innings was built on a strong 82-run partnership between RD Rickelton (44 off 33) and a blistering 59 off 38 from WG Jacks. SRH's NT Ellis struck twice in the 14th over to slow the momentum, but a fiery finish from Jay Gohil (43* off 24) and Sameer Rizvi (34* off 16) powered LSG to a formidable 192/4.

In reply, SRH's chase was anchored by a brilliant 80 off 43 from opener P Simran Singh. He kept SRH ahead of the rate, but his dismissal by H Ssenyondo in the 16th over triggered a dramatic collapse. LSG's death bowlers, led by a magnificent spell from JC Archer (4/25), took 5 wickets for just 13 runs in the final 3 overs.
