# 🏏 Match 47 – MI vs SRH: Spin Web and a Chase Masterclass! 🕸️

MI won the toss and elected to bat on a spin-friendly pitch. They got off to a flying start, but the introduction of KH Pandya turned the tide. He weaved a web around the MI batters, picking up a crucial 4-wicket haul to restrict them to a modest total of 187. Abhishek Sharma top-scored with a gritty 63, but the middle order crumbled under the spin pressure.

Chasing 188, SRH lost early wickets but found their anchor in KL Rahul. He played a composed yet aggressive knock, stitching a vital partnership with VR Iyer. The pair navigated the spin maze and accelerated at the death, taking SRH home with 9 balls to spare. It was a clinical chase that showcased their depth and composure.
