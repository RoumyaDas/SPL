📄 **Match 48: MI vs LSG – A Record Chase in the Death Over**

On a pace-friendly MI pitch, LSG won the toss and inserted MI. The decision backfired early as Tushar Raheja (51 off 19) launched a breathtaking assault – 9 fours and 2 sixes – racing MI to 55/0 in 4 overs. After his dismissal, Urvil Patel (50 off 46) anchored patiently while Mitch Marsh (67* off 42) accelerated in the death, propelling MI to 196/3. LSG’s bowlers bled 96 runs in the last 9 overs.

Chasing 197, Shubman Gill (69 off 40) and Finn Allen (89 off 58) stitched a monumental 148-run opening stand – the highest of the season. Allen was flawless, striking 7 fours and 4 sixes. Even after losing Gill and Mousley, LSG needed 22 off 12. Abishek Porel (8 off 4) and Sameer Rizvi (14 off 5) kept their nerve. Rizvi smashed a six off the 3rd ball of the final over, then scampered a single. Porel pushed the winning runs with 2 balls to spare. LSG chased 197 with 2 wickets and 2 balls remaining. A classic.

**Winner:** LSG  
**Player of the Match:** FH Allen (LSG)
