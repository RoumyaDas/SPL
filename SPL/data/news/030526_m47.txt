📄 **Match 47: SRH vs PBKS – A Last-Ball Finish for the Ages**

SRH won the toss and put PBKS in on a balanced pitch. PBKS stumbled early, losing Seifert (10) and Rana (8) inside the powerplay. Vansh Bedi (37* off 17) and Sanvir Singh (33* off 15) smashed 45 runs in the last 20 balls, dragging PBKS to 185/6. A brilliant recovery.

Chasing 186, SRH openers Sahil Parakh (53 off 32) and P Simran Singh (62 off 47) put on 94 for the first wicket – a calm, masterful stand. SRH won on the final delivery. No balls left. Unreal.

A heist. A classic. A last-ball thriller. 🏏
