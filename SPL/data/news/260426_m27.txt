**Match S07M027: RCB vs CSK – A Tie for the Ages!**

On a spin-friendly RCB pitch, CSK opted to bowl first after losing the toss. RCB’s PowerPlay was explosive – Abhishek Pathak smashed 33 off 14, but Gayle fell early. The middle order stuttered against Farooqi’s clever left-arm seam, losing wickets in clusters. A late flurry from JM Sharma (27 off 19) and Chirag Jani (14* off 8) dragged RCB to 185/8 – a fighting total but under par on a tricky surface.

CSK’s chase began disastrously: Rahane for a golden duck. Enter Tilak Varma. He anchored the innings with a magnificent 72 off 51, stitching a 100-run stand with Klaasen (58 off 38). Needing 14 off the final over, Maharaj dismissed Gaikwad, but Kotian launched a six and scrambled two runs to level scores. The last ball yielded one run – but CSK finished at 185/5. Exactly tied. RCB couldn’t defend, CSK couldn’t chase further. A rare, nerve-shredding tie.
