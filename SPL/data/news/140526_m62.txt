🕸️ **SPL S07, Match 62: LSG vs MI – A Spin Web That Choked the Life Out of MI**

LSG won the toss and opted to bowl first on a spin-friendly surface. And boy, did their spinners deliver. **H Ssenyondo (4/21)** and **Kuldeep Yadav (2/29)** spun a web around MI's middle order. Urvil Patel fought a lone battle, remaining unbeaten on 85 off 55, but saw wickets tumble at the other end. MI hobbled to **167/8**, a total that looked 30 runs short.

Chasing 168, LSG lost two early wickets but **Shubman Gill (38 off 19)** and **Abishek Porel (75* off 49)** steadied the ship. Then came a dramatic collapse — LSG slipped from 143/3 to 157/8, losing 5 wickets for just 14 runs. **Ehsan Khan (3/33)** and Shivam Shukla (2/29) nearly pulled off a miracle. But Porel held his nerve, and with 2 runs needed off the last over, he smashed a boundary to seal a nerve-shredding **1-wicket win**.
