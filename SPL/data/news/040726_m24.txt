# 🏏 SPL S08 - Match 24: LSG vs CSK

## A Stubbs Special Sets Up a Nail-Biting Chase! 🔥

CSK's innings was built around two magnificent knocks. **V Kohli** (76 off 49) and **T Stubbs** (82* off 52) stitched together a magnificent partnership, rescuing CSK from a precarious 34/3 and propelling them to a formidable 186/4. Their elegant strokeplay and intelligent running kept the scoreboard ticking at a rapid pace.

In response, LSG lost **Shubman Gill** early, but **DP Conway** (45 off 30) and **Abishek Porel** (47* off 27) kept the chase alive. Just when the required rate was climbing, **HH Pandya** (31* off 11) walked in and played a sensational cameo, smashing boundaries at will to turn the tide dramatically. The match went down to the wire, but Pandya's brutal hitting in the final over sealed a thrilling victory for LSG with 2 balls to spare. 💪
