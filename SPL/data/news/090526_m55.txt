# 🏏 SPL S07, Match 55: DC vs KKR – Spin Twins Strangle KKR in a Low-Scoring Thriller

The pitch at DC was a spinner's paradise, offering 2.4x more assistance to spin than pace. KKR won the toss and elected to bowl first, a decision that initially seemed justified as their bowlers kept things tight.

DC's innings never truly exploded. **Glenn Maxwell (41 off 25)** and **Aiden Markram (32)** provided a brisk start, but KKR's spinners, led by **Dishank Rathi (2/25)** and **Pulkit Narang (1/25)**, choked the middle overs. A late cameo from **Tarang Gohel (36* off 14)** pushed DC to a modest 177/6, a total that felt 15-20 runs short on a tricky surface.

KKR's chase began like a freight train. **Jake Fraser-McGurk (42 off 14)** smashed six boundaries in the first two overs, but **Ashwin Shelke** cut him short with a sharp LBW. Enter **Harsh Dubey (4/25)** and **Aiden Markram (3/18)**. The duo spun a web, picking wickets in clusters. **Chris Green (53)** fought valiantly, but KKR kept losing partners. From 42/0, they slumped to 172/9, falling short by 5 runs in a dramatic collapse.

**Result:** DC wins by 5 runs  
**Player of the Match:** Aiden Markram (DC) – for his all-round brilliance (32 runs & 3/18)
