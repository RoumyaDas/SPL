# 🏏 SPL S08 - Match 22: RCB vs MI

## A Taide Masterclass Seals a Thrilling Chase! 💥

MI's innings was a tale of two halves. After a disastrous start losing **Urvil Patel** for a golden duck, **Abhishek Tanwar** (52 off 28) and **MR Marsh** (48 off 35) staged a remarkable recovery, stitching together a crucial partnership to propel MI to a competitive 172.

Chasing 173, RCB lost **CH Gayle** early, but **Atharva Taide** (81 off 49) played a breathtaking knock, anchoring the chase with elegance and power. He found able support from **Priyansh Arya** (25 off 16) and **RM Patidar** (46* off 32), who finished the job in style. Despite a late wobble, RCB held their nerve and chased down the target with 12 balls to spare, securing a well-deserved victory. 🌟
