**🏏 SPL S07, Match 3: KKR vs CSK – Starc’s Five-Star Show Sinks CSK**

A chaotic, high-voltage clash saw KKR post a massive 224/8 after recovering from 6/2. Cameron Green (56 off 34) and Ishan Kishan (41 off 28) steadied the ship, but the real carnage came from D Wiese (47* off 15) and Ramandeep Singh (41 off 21), who looted 103 runs in the last 7 overs. Debutant Geet Puri (4/37) bowled well for CSK but couldn’t stop the bleeding.

Chasing 225, CSK’s openers struggled against the fiery MA Starc. Tilak Varma (72 off 38) and B Sai Sudharsan (54 off 35) added 102 runs, but the required rate kept climbing. Then Starc returned to rip through the lower order, finishing with a stunning 5/24 — his second five-wicket haul in SPL history. CSK folded for 172 in 19 overs, handing KKR a massive 52-run win.
