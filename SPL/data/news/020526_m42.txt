🏏 **SPL S07, Match 42: KKR vs LSG – Green Powers KKR Home in a Nail-Biter**

LSG won the toss and elected to bat first on a spin-friendly KKR pitch. Openers Shubman Gill and Finn Allen got off to a flying start, putting on 49 before Allen fell. Gill then anchored the innings with a sublime 72 off 41 (10 fours, 1 six), but wickets kept tumbling around him. After Gill's departure at 126/3, LSG's middle order struggled – only Jay Gohil's late assault of 33* off 13 balls dragged them to a respectable 187/8. Sandeep Lamichhane starred with the ball for KKR, claiming 3/43.

The chase began in breathtaking fashion – Jake Fraser-McGurk smashed 22 off just 7 balls (4 fours, 1 six) in the first over alone. After his wicket, Cameron Green walked in and played the anchor role to perfection. Green (59 off 41, 9 fours) and Ishan Kishan (65* off 47, 4 fours, 2 sixes) added 83 runs for the second wicket. With 21 needed off the last two overs, Kishan exploded – hitting a six and a four off H Ssenyondo in the 19th over. Wayne Madsen (42* off 20, 3 sixes) provided the finishing touches as KKR chased down 188 with 5 balls to spare.

**Winner:** KKR  
**Player of the Match:** C Green (KKR) – for his match-winning 59 off 41 and 2/24 with the ball.
