# 🏏 SPL Season 08, Match 9 – CSK vs KKR

In a high-stakes thriller that swung like a pendulum, **KKR** held their nerve to defend 209 against a resurgent CSK, clinching a thrilling 7-run victory.

KKR's innings was built around a blazing start from J Fraser-McGurk (57 off 23) and a gritty anchor from Ishan Kishan (74 off 47). After a cautious PowerPlay, the duo accelerated brilliantly, stitching together a crucial partnership. Though wickets fell in the middle overs, cameos from Akhil Singh Rawat (19 off 10) and Shahbaz Ahmed (20 off 9) propelled KKR to a formidable 209/6.

CSK's chase started sluggishly, losing F du Plessis and V Kohli early. Tilak Varma (26) and KK Nair (46) tried to steady the ship, but the required rate kept climbing. Enter H Klaasen (43) and RD Gaikwad (38) — they injected life into the chase with some exquisite hitting. The equation came down to 19 off the final over. Shahbaz Ahmed held his nerve brilliantly, picking up two wickets and conceding just 11 runs, as CSK finished at 202/7.

A masterclass in death bowling from KKR — the game was won in the final over.

**Player of the Match:** C Green – 3/36 in a spell that broke the back of CSK's chase.
