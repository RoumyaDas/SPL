# 🏏 SPL Season 08, Match 10 – MI vs GT

In a low-scoring thriller that went down to the wire, **GT** defended 193 in spectacular fashion, edging past MI by just 4 runs in a tense finish.

GT's innings was a tale of two halves — a solid PowerPlay from SV Samson (46) and JP Inglis (36) laid the foundation, but MI's bowlers struck back fiercely in the middle overs. JR Hazlewood (3/29) and Sunil Kumar (3/19) choked the scoring, reducing GT to 129/7 by the 15th over. Enter Ashutosh Sharma — his blistering 46 off 22 balls, combined with J Overton's 18 off 10, dragged GT to a competitive 193 all out.

MI's chase began disastrously with Urvil Patel departing for a golden duck. But Tushar Raheja (57 off 23) and Abhishek Sharma (65 off 52) resurrected the innings with a magnificent partnership. The required rate climbed steadily, and despite late fireworks from Abhishek Tanwar (22 off 7), MI fell agonizingly short. B Muzarabani (3/40) and Ashutosh Sharma (2/24) held their nerve in the death overs, sealing a nail-biting 4-run victory.

A classic case of momentum shifts — GT held their nerve when it mattered most.

**Player of the Match:** Ashutosh Sharma – 46 off 22 with the bat and 2/24 with the ball, a true all-round hero.
