🏆 SPL S07 – QUALIFIER 2 | GT vs RCB | RCB Home Ground (Pace: 2.6x)

TOSS: GT won the toss and elected to BOWL first on a pace-friendly wicket.

FIRST INNINGS – THE GAYLE STORM & MANENTI'S REDEMPTION

RCB openers walked in with intent. CH Gayle was in ominous touch – he smashed 66 off 28 (10 fours, 2 sixes) before falling to Harshit Rana. Priyansh Arya (25 off 16) fell to Linde. PWH de Silva (1) and Salil Arora (27 off 10, 2 sixes) came and went. At 127/4, RCB looked vulnerable. But RM Patidar (16) and JM Sharma (19) kept the scoreboard moving. Then came the collapse – Chirag Jani (7), KA Maharaj (0) fell quickly. At 167/8, RCB seemed headed for a sub-180 total. Enter HJ Manenti. The bowler-turned-batter smashed 14 off 12 (1 four, 1 six) and found unlikely support from SS Ghosh (10 off 4, 1 six). RCB crawled to 192/9. J Overton starred with 4/35 (4.0). GF Linde (2/29) and Ashutosh Sharma (2/30) chipped in.

SECOND INNING – THE MANENTI MASTERCLASS

GT needed 193. SV Samson (53 off 32, 6 fours, 4 sixes) and JP Inglis (57 off 30, 3 fours, 4 sixes) added 107 for the first wicket – a chase-defining partnership. At 111/0 in the 10th over, GT was cruising. Then the wheels fell off. Samson was run out. Inglis was run out. Naman Dhir (5) fell to Manenti. Ashutosh Sharma (11) fell to Ghosh. R Parag (13) fell to Manenti. RK Singh (7) fell to Manenti. J Overton (5) fell to Ghosh. GF Linde (3) and Harshit Rana (0) fell to Manenti. HJ Manenti ran through the middle and lower order – 6/24 in 4 overs (economy 6.0). GT collapsed from 111/0 to 178/9. RCB won by 14 runs.

A Gayle special. A Manenti fifer. RCB march to the Final.
