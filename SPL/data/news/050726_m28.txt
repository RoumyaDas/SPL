**Match 28 – DC vs RCB**: A Run-Fest for the Ages! 💥

On a pace-friendly track, RCB piled on a monstrous 238, powered by a majestic 88* off 38 from Abhishek Pathak and a brilliant 82 from Priyansh Arya. The DC bowlers looked helpless as boundaries rained down, with only JJ Bumrah and MJ Owen managing to pick up a couple of wickets each.

Chasing 239, DC knew they had to go big from the start. GJ Maxwell played a captain's knock of 87 off 44, setting the platform, while SA Yadav contributed a vital 63. The equation came down to the final few overs, and with the required rate climbing, N Sindhu (48 off 14) and Tarang Gohel (19 off 5) produced a sensational finish. They smashed 24 runs in the 19th over and then sealed the chase in the 20th with a flurry of boundaries, snatching victory with 4 balls to spare. It was a chase for the ages, leaving the crowd in sheer disbelief.
