# 🏏 SPL Season 08, Match 8 – DC vs LSG

In a run-fest that had everything — elegance, brutality, and a nerve-shredding finish — **DC** chased down 211 with three balls to spare, handing LSG their first loss of the season. The Feroz Shah Kotla lived up to its reputation as a batting paradise, with both teams tearing into the bowling with utter disdain.

LSG, put in to bat, rode on a breathtaking **96 off 51 from Shubman Gill**, who looked in complete control right from the first over. His innings was a masterclass in timing and placement, laced with 10 fours and 4 sixes. Despite losing FH Allen and DR Mousley early, Gill found an able partner in Abishek Porel, who anchored the innings with a composed 44 off 32. The real carnage, however, came in the death overs. Sameer Rizvi (36 off 14) and Hardik Pandya (17 off 5) absolutely decimated the DC bowlers, smashing 44 runs off the last two overs to propel LSG to a formidable 210/4.

Defending 211 was always going to be a mountain to climb, but DC’s openers made light work of the chase. **Tarang Gohel** played the innings of his life, smashing a sensational **98 off just 46 balls**, missing his century by the barest of margins. His knock was a blend of brute force and finesse, with 7 fours and 4 towering sixes. Glenn Maxwell provided the perfect foil with a quickfire 41 off 29, setting the platform. Though LSG struck back with quick wickets of Maxwell and SA Yadav, DC kept their composure. N Sindhu's blistering 37 off 18 sealed the deal, as DC cruised home with 3 balls to spare, finishing at 211/2.

A classic T20 shootout — LSG threw everything they had, but DC’s relentless batting depth proved too hot to handle.

**Player of the Match:** Tarang Gohel (DC) – 98 off 46, the architect of a record chase.
