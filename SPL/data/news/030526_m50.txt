🏏 **SPL S07, Match 45: GT vs RR – A Nail-Biting Chase in the Desert**

RR won the toss and chose to bowl first on a pitch that promised something for everyone. GT’s openers started brightly, with JP Inglis and Sanju Samson putting on 33 before Karan Lal ripped out Inglis’s off stump. Two balls later, Stephen removed Naman Dhir, and GT were wobbling at 38/2. Tim David and Samson then rebuilt with a flurry of boundaries, but just as Samson (39 off 23) looked set, Zeeshan Ansari took a stunning caught-and-bowled to break the stand. Wickets kept tumbling, and at 128/6 in the 15th over, GT seemed in trouble. Enter V Nigam. The young hitter smashed a breathtaking 37 off just 20 balls, clubbing four sixes to drag GT to a competitive 183/7. Zeeshan and Karan Lal finished with two wickets each.

In reply, RR’s chase was a statement. Yashasvi Jaiswal (32 off 15) and Ryan Rickelton (32 off 16) exploded out of the blocks – Harshit Rana was taken for 19 in his second over and Jaiswal fell, but the damage was done. After Rickelton departed at 64/2, Aniket Verma walked in and played a blinder. He and Dube (22) nudged before Aniket exploded: 41 off 17 balls (3 fours, 3 sixes), treating NKR and Parag with disdain. Even after Dube fell, Glenn Phillips (35* off 15) took over, finishing with back-to-back boundaries to seal the chase with 2.5 overs to spare. GT’s bowlers had no answers – Harshit Rana and Sufiyan Muqeem took two each, but the target was chased down with surgical precision.

**Winner:** RR  
**Player of the Match:** Aniket Verma (RR) – for his match-defining 41 off 17 in a high-pressure chase.
