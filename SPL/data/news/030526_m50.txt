📄 **Match 50: CSK vs GT – A Spin-Fueled Collapse at Chepauk**

GT won the toss and elected to bowl on a balanced Chennai pitch. CSK’s openers Gaikwad (63 off 39) and Sai Sudharsan (13) started briskly before Sudharsan fell. Tilak Varma (35) and Gaikwad added 74, but Gaikwad’s dismissal triggered a middle-over wobble. Enter Klaasen (40 off 19) and KK Nair (35 off 15) – they tore into GT’s bowlers, smashing 56 off 21 balls. A chaotic death over saw Linde (4/40) and Overton (3/36) trigger a collapse of 4 wickets in 6 balls, yet CSK still posted 202/9 – a formidable total.

Chasing 203, GT lost Inglis (0) first ball. Samson (27 off 12) attacked but fell to Chahal (3/31). Naman Dhir (80 off 41) fought a lone battle, hammering 7 fours and 5 sixes, but wickets kept tumbling at the other end. van der Merwe (3/46) and Sonu Yadav (2/29) choked the chase. Dhir was the last man out in the 19th over, and GT were bundled out for 188. A clinical CSK defense – their spinners squeezing the life out of the chase. 🏏

**Winner:** CSK  
**Player of the Match:** Naman Dhir (GT)
