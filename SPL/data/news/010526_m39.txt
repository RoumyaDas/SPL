🏏 **SPL S07 Match 39: MI vs GT – Record Chase Silences GT's Giant Total**

GT elected to bat first on a pace-friendly pitch, but they lost JP Inglis for a golden duck. **Naman Dhir** (76 off 47) and a late blitz from **V Nigam** (42* off 17) powered GT to a massive 207/6. MI’s Abhishek Tanwar grabbed 3/55, but GT looked safe.

Chasing 208, MI lost an early wicket, but **Abhishek Sharma** played a career-defining knock—an unbeaten 76 off just 35 balls (10 fours, 2 sixes). He got able support from Urvil Patel (42) and a blazing cameo from MR Marsh (37 off 14). MI chased down the target with 4 balls to spare, winning by 6 wickets in a run-fest. GT bowlers had no answer to Abhishek’s onslaught.
