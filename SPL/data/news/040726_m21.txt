# 🏏 SPL S08 - Match 21: RR vs PBKS

## A Run-Fest with a Thrilling Finish! 🔥

The RR fortress witnessed a high-octane clash as PBKS set a daunting 196-run target, but RR chased it down with panache in the final over.

PBKS's innings was a tale of two halves. An explosive start from **V Suryavanshi** (43 off 34) and **PHKD Mendis** (47 off 26) propelled them to a strong total. However, a late collapse, triggered by **L Ngidi**'s brilliant death bowling (3/38), restricted them to 195/8.

In reply, RR lost early wickets but **S Dube** (48 off 24) played a masterclass, anchoring the chase with a flurry of boundaries. Despite a mid-innings wobble that saw them lose wickets in clusters, **GD Phillips** (46 off 25) and **KA Pollard** (26 off 12) provided the late fireworks to keep the run-rate in check.

Needing 15 runs off the last two overs, **Vishnu Vinod** smashed two massive sixes off AR Ramjani to seal the victory with 7 balls to spare, completing a remarkable chase. It was a game that swung back and forth, but RR held their nerve to emerge victorious.
