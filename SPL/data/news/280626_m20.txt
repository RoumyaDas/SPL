# 🏏 SPL S08 – Match 20: CSK vs DC

In a high-octane run-fest dominated by spin-friendly conditions, DC emerged victorious in a thrilling shootout against CSK, defending a massive total despite a valiant chase.

DC, put in to bat, lost early wickets but Suryakumar Yadav anchored the innings with a breathtaking 98 off just 51 balls. He found able support from Sunny Sandhu (42), and a sensational late assault from Andre Russell — who smashed 34 off 12 balls with 4 sixes — propelled DC to an imposing 222/5.

CSK's chase got off to a rocky start with Virat Kohli falling for a duck. But Tilak Varma (97) and Ajinkya Rahane (57) stitched together a stunning 133-run partnership to keep CSK in the hunt. However, the required rate kept climbing, and despite a late cameo from Ruturaj Gaikwad (32 off 11), CSK fell agonizingly short, finishing at 216/5 — just 6 runs away from a miraculous victory.
