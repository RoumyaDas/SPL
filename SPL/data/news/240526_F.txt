🏆 SPL S07 Final – Match 74: RCB vs SRH

A finale worthy of the stage. A spin-heavy surface, two explosive batting lineups, and relentless pressure from ball one till the final over. 🔥

After SRH chose to bowl first, RCB came out swinging. Chris Gayle survived an early storm and turned the Powerplay into pure destruction, while Atharva Taide’s fearless cameo pushed the momentum even further. SRH managed to strike back through Nathan Ellis and Sai Kishore, but just when things looked slightly under control, Abhishek Pathak unleashed absolute chaos.

The middle overs became the Pathak show. He dismantled spin, punished anything short, and exploded brutally in the death overs. His late assault, combined with Gayle’s classy 63, carried RCB to a massive 234/4 in the final. On a pitch expected to aid spin heavily, RCB had somehow made it look flat.

But SRH refused to back down.

The chase began at breakneck speed before early wickets put them under pressure. KL Rahul then stepped into the spotlight. Calm when needed, aggressive when required, he stitched together a stunning counterattack with Kartik Sharma before Venky Iyer arrived and turned the game into a thriller.

The Rahul-Iyer partnership brought SRH roaring back into the contest. Boundaries kept flowing, the required rate stayed alive, and suddenly the final started tilting again. Every over felt like a momentum swing. Every wicket changed the atmosphere.

With 18 needed off the final over, SRH still believed.

But Chris Gayle had one last twist left in the script. The veteran removed KL Rahul immediately after being smashed for six, then cleaned up Hardik Pandya the very next ball to crush SRH’s hopes. RCB held their nerve in the biggest game of the season and sealed the SPL S07 title in dramatic fashion. 🏆
