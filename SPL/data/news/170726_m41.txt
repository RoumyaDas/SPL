# 🏏 SPL S08 - Match 41: RCB vs GT

**Toss:** GT won the toss and chose to bat first on a pace-friendly pitch.

The first innings was a rollercoaster of explosive hitting and dramatic wickets. GT's innings started with a flurry of boundaries before **M Pathirana** struck twice in quick succession to remove the openers. However, **SV Samson** stood firm like a rock, anchoring the innings with a classy 89. He found able partners in **Naman Dhir** and **D Brevis**, who kept the scoreboard ticking. A late cameo from **V Nigam**, who smashed 26 off just 7 balls, propelled GT to a massive total. Samson's knock, studded with 14 boundaries, was the highlight of the GT innings.

In response, RCB's chase began with absolute carnage. Openers **Atharva Taide** and **Priyansh Arya** put on a record-breaking 162-run partnership, treating the GT bowlers with utter disdain. Taide's elegant 77 was complemented perfectly by Arya's aggressive 96, who fell agonizingly short of a well-deserved century. With the required rate climbing, **CH Gayle** walked in and produced a vintage performance, smashing four consecutive sixes in the final over to seal the chase with two balls to spare. It was a display of pure power-hitting that left the GT bowlers shell-shocked.

RCB wins by 8 wickets, completing a sensational chase!
