🏏 **SPL S07, Match 43: CSK vs PBKS – Kotian's All-Round Heroics Seal a Thriller**

PBKS won the toss and elected to bowl first on a spin-heavy CSK pitch. The home side lost Tilak Varma for a golden duck and Sai Sudharsan early, but Ruturaj Gaikwad (39 off 24) and Heinrich Klaasen (34 off 23) steadied the ship. After both fell, KK Nair unleashed carnage – an unbeaten 74 off just 36 balls with 7 sixes. Tanush Kotian then provided the perfect finish, smashing 43 off 17 (4 sixes) as CSK raced to 198/6. Utkarsh Singh (2/23) and CV Varun (2/26) were the pick of the PBKS bowlers.

The chase began explosively – Vaibhav Suryavanshi hammered 46 off 16 balls (9 fours, 1 six) as PBKS raced to 58/1 inside 4 overs. After his departure, SP Narine (46 off 23) kept the momentum going. But CSK's spinners fought back brilliantly – Fazalhaq Farooqi (2/30), RE van der Merwe (2/34), and Tanush Kotian (2/24) choked the run flow. Despite Shashank Singh's late 27 off 14, PBKS fell short by 19 runs. YS Chahal cleaned up the tail to trigger a dramatic collapse.

**Winner:** CSK  
**Player of the Match:** Tanush Kotian (CSK) – for his explosive 43* off 17 and crucial 2/24.
