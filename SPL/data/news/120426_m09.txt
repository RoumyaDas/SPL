📝 **Match Summary – SPL S07, Match 9: RR vs PBKS**

Toss: RR opted to bat first on a spin minefield (2.6x spin assistance).

What followed was absolute carnage. RR’s innings never left the driveway – Jaiswal and Salt both fell for ducks in the first over. **AR Ramjani** (3/20) and **Utkarsh Singh** (2/2) turned the pitch into a nightmare. Only Rickelton (21) and Aniket Verma (29) showed fight, but RR crashed to **91 all out** in 11.3 overs. Four batters scored zero. The lowest total of the season so far.

Chasing 92, PBKS lost Narine (27) and Suryavanshi (31) after a brisk start, but the target was too small. A few nervy moments – 5 wickets fell – but **Shashank Singh** (13*) and **T Stubbs** (18*) knocked off the runs in the 12th over. A 5-wicket win that felt like a formality after that bowling demolition.

Spin ruled. RR had no answer.
