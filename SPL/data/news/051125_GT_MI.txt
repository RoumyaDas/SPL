GT won the toss and elected to bowl first. MI got off to a shaky start when Abhishek Sharma fell for a golden duck in the very first over. Despite some brief resistance from Josh Inglis (37 off 19) and Riyan Parag (23 off 9), GT's innings eventually collapsed. They were bowled out for 173 in 18.2 overs, handing MI a comprehensive 37-run victory.
