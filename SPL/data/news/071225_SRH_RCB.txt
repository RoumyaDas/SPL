SPL Season 5 | Match 89: SRH vs RCB at SRH

SRH won the toss and elected to bat first. P Simran Singh gave them a quick start with 24, but wickets fell regularly. KL Rahul anchored the innings with an unbeaten 67, supported by Nithish Kumar Reddy's 50* off 31 balls, helping SRH reach 179/4. For RCB, MG Erasmus took 2/43.

In response, RCB's chase was led by PD Salt's 48 and PWH de Silva's 41. Shadab Khan's spin (3/22) threatened to derail the chase, but RA Jadeja's explosive 22* off just 6 balls finished the game with 11 balls to spare, securing a 3-wicket win for RCB.
