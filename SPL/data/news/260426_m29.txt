**Match S07M029: LSG vs RR – Mousley’s Masterclass Anchors a Record Chase**

On a pace-friendly Lucknow track, RR opted to bat first. After early jitters, S Dube (71 off 42) and Aniket Verma (57 off 34) stitched a magnificent 120-run stand to propel RR to 190. But LSG's bowlers struck back in the death – Arshdeep Singh (4/38) and Mohammad Amir (3/27) triggered a collapse, bowling RR out on the final ball.

Chasing 191, LSG lost Gill early. Enter DR Mousley. The young batter played the innings of his life – an unbeaten 98 off 60, combining calm with aggression. He found able support from SR Watson (36 off 26) and then Abishek Porel (57* off 29), who exploded in the death. LSG chased down the target with two balls to spare, losing only two wickets. A heist, headlined by Mousley’s near-century.
