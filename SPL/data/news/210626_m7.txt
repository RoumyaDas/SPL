# 🏏 SPL Season 08, Match 7 – SRH vs PBKS

In a run-fest that kept the crowd on the edge of their seats, **SRH** defended 217 in a nerve-shredding final over, edging out PBKS by just 4 runs.

SRH's innings was a masterclass in batting depth. P Simran Singh (72 off 43) laid a solid foundation, while Kartik Sharma (57 off 34) kept the momentum flowing. But the real carnage came from VR Iyer, whose blistering 75 off just 32 balls — decorated with 9 fours and 3 sixes — propelled SRH to a daunting 217/3. PBKS's bowlers had no answers to the late assault, with JJ Smit (2/37) being the only bright spot.

The chase began with fireworks as TL Seifert (89 off 45) and V Suryavanshi (10 off 5) gave PBKS a flying start. However, wickets kept tumbling at regular intervals, bringing PHKD Mendis to the crease. Mendis played a gritty knock of 66 off 34, keeping PBKS in the hunt. With 16 runs needed off the final over, Rasikh Salam held his nerve, picking up two crucial wickets and conceding just 11 runs. PBKS finished at 213/9, falling agonizingly short.

A classic T20 thriller — SRH held their nerve when it mattered most.

**Player of the Match:** TL Seifert – 89 off 45, a magnificent knock that nearly pulled off the impossible.
