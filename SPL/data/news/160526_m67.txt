# 🏏 SPL S07 Match 67: MI vs DC – Spin Web Traps the Chase

MI won the toss and sent DC in on a pitch tailor-made for spin. Maxwell, in his element, raced to a brisk 58, stitching a steady opening stand with Markram. Sindhu then took over, playing a composed 50-run knock. But MI’s spinners – led by Shivam Shukla (3/27) – kept chipping away. Just when DC looked set for a bigger total, Parth Chandan’s late fireworks (34 off 18) dragged them to 202/7. A fighting total, but not insurmountable.

In reply, MI’s openers exploded – 17 off the first over. Then the spin trap snapped shut. Ashwin Shelke (3/48) and Harsh Dubey (2/30) squeezed the life out of the chase. Urvil Patel fought a lone battle with a gritty 67, but wickets tumbled in clusters. From 108/3, MI crumbled to 177 all out. Bumrah and Markram mopped up the tail as DC spun their way to a 25-run victory on a treacherous turner.

**Winner:** DC  
**Player of the Match:** Urvil Patel (MI) – for a valiant 67 under pressure.
