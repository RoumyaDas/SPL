# 🏏 SPL S08 - Match 45: PBKS vs CSK

**Toss:** PBKS won the toss and chose to bat first on a pitch tailor-made for spinners.

The first innings saw an explosive start from PBKS openers **V Suryavanshi** and **TL Seifert**, who put on a blistering 70-run partnership. Seifert reached his half-century in style before becoming the first victim of a sensational bowling display. CSK's spinners, led by debutant **Prashant Solanki**, then triggered a remarkable collapse. Solanki's magical spell, supported brilliantly by **RE van der Merwe**, reduced PBKS from 74/1 to a precarious position. A late cameo from **Shashank Singh** helped PBKS post a fighting total.

In response, CSK's chase turned into a nightmare from the very first ball. The spin-friendly pitch became a minefield as PBKS bowlers **CV Varun** and **Shashank Singh** ripped through the CSK batting order. Varun struck twice in his first over, removing both openers, and the wickets kept tumbling like dominoes. CSK were bundled out for a paltry total, completely outplayed by the spin quartet of PBKS, who won the match in comprehensive fashion.

PBKS wins by a massive margin of 100 runs!
