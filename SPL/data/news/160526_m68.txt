# 🏏 SPL S07 Match 68: SRH vs GT – Inglis and Parag Pile On, Linde Spins SRH Out

GT opted to bat first on a balanced surface. Samson departed early for a fiery 29, but Inglis and Parag then staged a batting masterclass. Inglis, dropped early in his innings, made SRH pay dearly, falling agonizingly one short of a century – 99 off 51 balls with 14 fours and 2 sixes. Parag supported beautifully with a fluent 78. Late blows from Brevis and Nigam propelled GT to a mammoth 236/4.

Chasing 237, SRH started briskly. Parakh slammed 56, and Pandya chipped in with 46. But the moment Pandya fell, the wheels came off. Linde (4/38) and Overton (3/37) sliced through the middle order. Buttler tried to resist with 31, but the asking rate ballooned. SRH folded for 191, falling 45 runs short in a one-sided contest.

**Winner:** GT  
**Player of the Match:** JP Inglis (GT) – for his breathtaking 99.
