**🔥 SPL Season 6 – Qualifier 2: Lucknow Super Giants vs Mumbai Indians – Match Summary**

In a high-pressure Qualifier 2 at Lucknow, Mumbai Indians pulled off a stunning heist to book their place in the final, thanks to a generational knock from Nicholas Pooran and a disciplined bowling performance on a spin-friendly surface.

Asked to bat first on a pitch assisting spin by 2.2x, MI found themselves in deep trouble at 95/5 in the 9th over. LSG's spinners, led by Mohammad Nabi (2/47) and K Kartikeya (1/26), had reduced them to rubble. But Nicholas Pooran had other ideas. In what turned out to be a season-defining innings, Pooran launched a brutal counterattack, smashing an unbeaten 88 off just 50 balls (11 fours, 4 sixes). He found late support from Shaheen Afridi (18 off 13) and JM Coles (15* off 18), dragging MI to a competitive 202/6.

Chasing 203, LSG's reply never gained momentum. R Sai Kishore (2/35) struck twice in an over, removing Shubman Gill and Will Jacks for a duck in the powerplay. Despite Shreyas Iyer's fighting 47 off 29 and Jos Buttler's 43* off 30, LSG kept losing wickets at regular intervals. JM Coles (2/31) and Shivam Shukla (2/39) cleaned up the middle order, and MI's spinners choked the chase. LSG finished at 169/8, falling short by 33 runs.

Pooran's magnificent 88* under pressure earned him the Player of the Match as MI stormed into the final.
