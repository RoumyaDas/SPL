🏏 **SPL S07 Match 38: LSG vs DC – Bumrah's Four-Fer, Then Sindhu & Gohel Steal the Show**

LSG opted to bat first on a pace-friendly pitch. Openers **Shubman Gill** (61 off 38) and **FH Allen** (50 off 33) laid a solid foundation with a 110-run stand. But **Jasprit Bumrah** (4/40) triggered a collapse, picking wickets at regular intervals to restrict LSG to 200/7. Shaheen Afridi chipped in with 2/32.

Chasing 201, DC openers went big. **Glenn Maxwell** smashed 61 off just 26 balls (9 fours, 2 sixes) to set the tone. After a mini-wobble, **Nishant Sindhu** (64* off 29) and **Tarang Gohel** (53* off 26) stitched an unbeaten 103-run stand, finishing the chase in just 16.3 overs. DC won by 7 wickets with 21 balls to spare. LSG bowlers were taken apart — Jacks conceded 53 in 3 overs, Amir went for 43 in 3.3.
