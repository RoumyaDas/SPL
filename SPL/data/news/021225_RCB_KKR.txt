Match 76, SPL S05 : RCB v KKR

In Match 76, RCB chose to bat first after winning the toss at home. Their innings never gained momentum, with only PP Shaw (51 off 42) and a quickfire 39 off 21 from PWH de Silva offering resistance. KKR's bowlers, led by a magnificent spell from YK Pathan (5/19), dismantled the middle and lower order. Pathan's five-wicket haul included three wickets in a single over, sparking a collapse where RCB lost 5 wickets for just 24 runs. They were bundled out for a below-par 146 in 17.2 overs.

Chasing 147, KKR openers started aggressively, with SP Narine smashing 29 off just 11 balls. Despite losing Stoinis and Narine early, LS Livingstone (27) and RM Patidar (38* off 20) steadied the chase. The finishing touches were applied by the Player of the Match, YK Pathan, who capped off his stellar day with an unbeaten 21 off 12 balls.
