# 🏏 SPL S07 Match 15: RR vs RCB – Abrar's 4/18 Wrecks RR's Chase

On a balanced pitch, RR opted to bowl first. RCB's debutant Abhishek Pathak (54 off 38) anchored the innings with Priyansh Arya (26). After a middle-overs lull, Salil Arora (65* off 28) and Rajat Patidar (47* off 19) exploded — smashing 82 runs in the last 5 overs. RR's bowlers S Dube (2/34) and Karan Lal (1/41) had no answer. RCB posted a mammoth 220/3.

Chasing 221, RR lost Rickelton (run out) and Kohli (18) early. Salt (57) and Dube (40) rebuilt, but wickets fell at crucial moments. Then came **Abrar Ahmed** (4/18 off 4 overs) — the mystery spinner tore through the middle order: Jaiswal, Phillips, Karan Lal, and the dangerous Aniket Verma (39) all fell to him. Keshav Maharaj (1/39) and de Silva (2/37) supported. RR collapsed from 160/4 to 206/8, falling 14 runs short. A stunning defensive bowling display on a flat pitch.

**Winner:** RCB  
**Player of the Match:** Abrar Ahmed (4/18)
