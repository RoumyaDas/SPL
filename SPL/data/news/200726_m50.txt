# 🏏 Match 50 – LSG vs PBKS: A Chase for the Ages! 🔥

PBKS won the toss and elected to bat on a pace-friendly pitch. They got off to a flier, with Tim Seifert and Vaibhav Suryavanshi putting on a rapid 74-run opening stand. Seifert smashed a quick 47, while Suryavanshi fell agonizingly short of a half-century on 49. A late flourish from Vansh Bedi (41*) and Shashank Singh (26*) propelled PBKS to a daunting total of 207.

Chasing 208, LSG openers Shubman Gill and Devon Conway provided a solid start. After Conway's dismissal, Ajay Rohera walked in and played the innings of his life. Alongside Abishek Porel, he orchestrated a brilliant chase, smashing boundaries at will. Rohera remained unbeaten on 71, while Porel scored a crucial 56. Hardik Pandya finished it off with a massive six, sealing a thrilling victory with an over to spare!
