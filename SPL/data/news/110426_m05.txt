**🏏 SPL S07, Match 5: SRH vs RR – Dube’s Masterclass Anchors a Nail-Biting Chase**

SRH opted to bat first on a balanced pitch and posted 195/8, thanks largely to KL Rahul’s classy 88 off 57. Karan Lal (3/39) was the pick of RR’s bowlers, but Rahul held one end together even as wickets fell around him.

Chasing 196, RR lost Jaiswal (19) and Kohli (1) early, but S Dube played the innings of his life. Unbeaten on 86 off 52, he found able support from Aniket Verma (45 off 25) and GD Phillips (27* off 11). The required rate climbed, but Dube kept his cool. With 2 needed off the final over, he calmly guided a two to seal a 6-wicket win with 5 balls to spare. A chase masterclass from the big man.
