**🏏 Match 70: MI vs RR – Summary**

In a run-chase masterclass at the Wankhede, Mumbai Indians gunned down 182 with an over to spare, riding on a sublime unbeaten 64 from WL Madsen. Asked to bat first, RR posted a competitive 181/6, powered by AB de Villiers' classy 51 and cameos from Porel (36) and McMullen (31). Noor Ahmad was exceptional for MI with 3/28.

In response, MI lost early wickets but found stability through Abhishek Sharma's fluent 48 and a match-winning 64* from Madsen, who anchored the chase with composure. Despite late strikes from KH Pandya and Rishad Hossain, Madsen held his nerve to see MI home with 8 balls to spare.

A clinical chase from MI, and WL Madsen walks away with the Player of the Match for his cool-headed knock.
