**🏏 SPL S07, Match 4: GT vs DC – Markram’s Six-Wicket Magic Steals a Thriller**

A run-fest saw DC pile up a monstrous 251/2, powered by sensational fifties from GJ Maxwell (74 off 42), Tarang Gohel (72 off 39), and a blazing SA Yadav (53* off 21). GT’s bowlers were carted all around, with V Nigam leaking 67 in his 4 overs.

Chasing 252, GT’s openers came out firing — JP Inglis smashed 77 off 31, and Naman Dhir added a gritty 82 off 47. At 161/2 in the 12th over, GT looked in control. But then AK Markram happened. He ran through the middle and lower order, claiming a stunning 6/40 — his maiden five-wicket haul in SPL. GT collapsed from 168/2 to 241 all out, falling just 10 runs short in a heartbreaker. Markram was unplayable, and DC escaped with a narrow 10-run win.
