**Match S07M028: KKR vs GT – Harshit Rana’s One-Man Show Overwhelms KKR**

KKR’s innings never recovered – from 60/1, they collapsed to 129 all out in 17.3 overs. Only Ishan Kishan (40 off 25) and Rohit Sharma (31 off 17) offered brief resistance.
