# 🏏 SPL S07 Match 11: MI vs KKR – A Nail-Biting Cliffhanger

Toss: MI won & chose to bowl first on a pitch tailor-made for spinners (1.8x assistance). KKR’s gamble to bat first backfired early when Duckett (8) holed out against Ehsan Khan. But then came a stunning 85* off 52 balls from Rohan Kunnummal — a debutant with no SPL record — who played with fearless maturity. Ishan Kishan (36) and Rohit Sharma (42) chipped in, but it was Kunnummal’s calculated aggression and a late flurry from D Wiese (14 off 8) that lifted KKR to 194/5. Ehsan Khan (3/46) and Noor Ahmad (2/22) kept MI in the hunt.

Chasing 195, MI’s nightmare began immediately: Abhishek Sharma (2) trapped LBW by Livingstone off the third ball. Urvil Patel (84* off 56) anchored brilliantly, but wickets fell at regular intervals. Tushar Raheja (32) and Smaran Ravichandran (38) tried to revive, but the asking rate ballooned. Needing 33 off 12, then 20 off the last over, MI fell just 7 runs short. Livingstone’s final-over wicket of Pooran (18 off 7) sealed the heist. KKR won by 6 runs in a spine-tingling finish.

**Winner:** KKR  
**Player of the Match:** Rohan Kunnummal (85* off 52, 7 fours, 4 sixes)
