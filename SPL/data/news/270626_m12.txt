# 🏏 Match 12 – CSK vs SRH: A Chase for the Ages

In a run-fest that showcased the true spirit of T20 cricket, SRH pulled off a stunning heist against CSK on a pitch that favored pace. Asked to bat first, CSK's innings was a tale of two halves – a wobbly start that saw them lose wickets in a cluster, followed by a blistering recovery in the death overs. Despite a brilliant bowling performance from SRH's pacers, Nathan Ellis and Rasikh Salam, who picked up three wickets each, CSK managed to post a competitive 171 thanks to some late fireworks from Roelof van der Merwe and Naveen-ul-Haq.

Chasing 172, SRH came out with all guns blazing. The openers, SU Parakh and P Simran Singh, tore into the CSK bowling, setting the tone for a mammoth chase. Although Simran Singh fell early, KL Rahul walked in and played a captain's knock, anchoring the innings with an unbeaten 61. Alongside him, Kartik Sharma played a sensational cameo of 48*, smashing boundaries at will to take the game away from CSK. The pair added an unbeaten 83 runs for the third wicket, guiding SRH to a comprehensive victory with 21 balls to spare. It was a masterclass in chasing, led by KL Rahul's class and the explosive hitting of Kartik Sharma, which sealed the win for SRH.
