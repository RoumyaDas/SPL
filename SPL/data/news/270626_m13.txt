🏏 Match 13 – KKR vs MI: A Rollercoaster of Wickets and Resilience

In a match that resembled a high-stakes game of cricket's roulette, KKR and MI served up a chaotic yet captivating contest where the fall of wickets was the only constant. MI won the toss and chose to bowl, a decision that paid immediate dividends as their pacers, led by Mohammed Shami, ripped through KKR's top order, reducing them to a nightmarish 24/5 within the powerplay. Just when it seemed KKR would be skittled for a paltry total, C Green stood tall amidst the rubble, crafting a resolute 57 to steady the ship, while Shahbaz Ahmed provided a quick-fire cameo. A late collapse saw KKR bowled out for a modest 130, with Shami's 3-wicket haul leading the charge.

The chase, however, proved that this pitch was a bowler's paradise. KKR's own bowlers, Suryansh Shedge and D Wiese, responded with venom, turning the game into a wicket-fest. MI's openers began with a boundary-blitz, but the introduction of these two wreaked havoc. Wickets tumbled in a procession, with a stunning collapse of 4 wickets for just 2 runs in a chaotic passage of play. Despite a valiant lower-order fightback from Siddhesh Veer and Abhishek Tanwar, the target proved elusive. KKR bundled MI out for 129 in the final over, clinching a thrilling 1-run victory in a match where the scoreboard rarely paused. It was a game of breathtaking twists, where the bowlers held all the aces, and C Green's all-round brilliance (57 with the bat and two crucial wickets) rightfully earned him the Player of the Match.
