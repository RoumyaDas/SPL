**🏆 SPL Season 6 – Qualifier 1: Mumbai Indians vs Royal Challengers Bangalore – Match Summary**

In a high-stakes Qualifier played on a spin-friendly track at RR, Royal Challengers Bangalore produced a clinical all-round performance to defeat Mumbai Indians by 25 runs and book their spot in the final.

Asked to bowl first, MI struck early but Priyansh Arya (53 off 28) launched a stunning counterattack. He was well-supported by the in-form Wanindu Hasaranga (46 off 31). However, it was the unheralded Salil Arora who stole the show, smashing a blistering 46* off just 27 balls to propel RCB to a formidable 198/7. Noor Ahmad was the pick of MI bowlers with a brilliant 3/31.

Chasing 199, MI openers Abhishek Sharma (45 off 22) and Atharva Taide (53 off 38) gave them a flying start, adding 74 in just 6.5 overs. But just as they looked in control, RCB's spinners struck back. Hasaranga, Harsh Dubey, and Ravi Teja shared six wickets between them, triggering a dramatic middle-order collapse. From a position of strength at 108/1, MI crumbled to 173/7, falling short by 25 runs.

RCB's spin trio choked the life out of MI's chase in the middle overs, and despite a late cameo from Shaheen Afridi, MI couldn't recover. Wanindu Hasaranga's all-round brilliance (46 runs & 2 wickets) earned him the Player of the Match as RCB sealed a memorable win.
