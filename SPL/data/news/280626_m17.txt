# 🏏 SPL S08 – Match 17: MI vs LSG

In a run-fest that had the scoreboard ticking like a pinball machine, LSG chased down a mammoth 210 with just one ball to spare, stealing victory in a nail-biting final over.

MI, asked to bat first, recovered from an early duck to post a towering 209/5. Rohan Kunnummal and Abhishek Sharma laid the foundation with a sparkling 140-run partnership, scoring 83 and 62 respectively. Mitchell Marsh then provided the death-over fireworks, smashing 42 off 23 to propel MI past 200.

The chase began with LSG openers Shubman Gill and DP Conway putting on a masterclass — stitching together a 153-run stand without breaking a sweat. Gill fell agonizingly short of a century, scoring 80, while Conway compiled a steady 73. But when both departed in quick succession, LSG stumbled, losing 5 wickets for just 20 runs. With 20 runs needed off the last over, D Forrester played the cameo of a lifetime, smashing two sixes and a three, before HH Pandya finished it off with a towering maximum to seal a dramatic 4-wicket win.
