# 🏏 SPL S08 – Match 16: SRH vs RR

In a high-voltage clash that had everything — stunning hitting, dramatic twists, and a chase for the ages — SRH pulled off a record-shattering heist against RR, chasing down 206 with 7 balls to spare.

RR, put in to bat first, got off to a blistering start thanks to Aniket Verma, who smashed three boundaries in the very first over. But just when he looked set, he fell for 23. PD Salt anchored the innings with a steady 34, while S Dube played the perfect sheet-anchor role, remaining unbeaten on 60. The real fireworks came from Kieron Pollard, who launched a brutal assault in the death overs, smashing 56 off just 19 balls — including 6 sixes — to propel RR to a mammoth 205/5.

The chase began in the worst possible way for SRH, losing P Simran Singh early for just 2, followed by the run-out of KL Rahul for a quick 5, leaving them reeling at 11/2. But then came the turning point. **Kartik Sharma** walked in and played one of the most destructive T20 knocks ever seen. He tore into the RR attack with a breathtaking 128 off just 50 balls, hitting 8 fours and 9 sixes. He found an able partner in SU Parakh, who made 39 before falling to Pollard. Despite a mini-collapse that saw SRH slip to 102/4, Kartik held his nerve and, alongside MP Stoinis, guided SRH past the finish line in emphatic fashion, sealing a thrilling 5-wicket victory and the Player of the Match award for his heroic century.
