# 🏏 SPL S07 Match 69: RCB vs LSG – Patidar and Pathak Orchestrate a Record Chase

LSG opted to bat first on a pace-friendly surface. Shubman Gill departed early for 13, but FH Allen played a majestic hand, smashing 76 off 41 balls with 5 fours and 5 sixes. Mousley (33) and Porel (38) kept the scoreboard ticking, while Sameer Rizvi's late fireworks (35* off 15) and Jay Gohil's cameo (15 off 5) propelled LSG to a daunting 221/5.

In response, RCB lost early wickets – Raghuwanshi (19), Harnoor (13), and de Silva (0) falling cheaply. At 50/3, the chase looked bleak. Enter Abhishek Pathak and Rajat Patidar. The duo stitched a breathtaking 142-run stand. Pathak scored a sublime 77 off 46. Patidar remained unbeaten on 86 off 47. Then came the final over carnage – Jitesh Sharma walked in at 192/4 with 30 needed off 12. He smashed 26 off just 5 balls (2 fours, 3 sixes) as RCB chased down 222 with 5 balls to spare. One of the great comebacks.

**Winner:** RCB  
**Player of the Match:** RM Patidar (RCB) – for his match-winning 86*.
