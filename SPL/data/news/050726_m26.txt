**Match 26 – RR vs KKR**: A Spin-Fueled Rollercoaster at the RR Fortress 🏟️

The pitch was a turner, and the spinners were the true architects of this contest. RR, opting to bowl first, saw Mohammad Ghazanfar and Atharva Ankolekar wreak havoc, claiming four wickets each. Despite a breathtaking 65 off 47 from Ishan Kishan, who looked set to guide RR to a massive total, the innings kept losing steam. Wickets tumbled in clusters, but a late cameo from WL Madsen (42 off 17) propelled them to a competitive 170.

The chase was a mirror image of carnage. Aniket Verma launched a fearsome counterattack, smashing a rapid 55 off 22, making the target look like a walk in the park. However, the wheels fell off spectacularly. Pulkit Narang and V Puthur spun a web of confusion, triggering a dramatic collapse. The RR middle-order wilted under pressure, losing their last six wickets for just 46 runs, handing KKR a stunning victory. The match swung like a pendulum, but the spinners ultimately determined the fate.
