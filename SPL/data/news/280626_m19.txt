# 🏏 SPL S08 – Match 19: KKR vs RCB

In a thrilling contest that ebbed and flowed with every over, RCB produced a clinical chase to hunt down 177 with two overs to spare, riding on a match-winning partnership.

KKR, put in to bat first, lost early wickets but found stability through Cameron Green's explosive 37 and a gritty 59 from Shreyas Iyer. However, a sensational bowling performance from **HJ Manenti** — who picked up 4 wickets for just 29 runs — triggered a dramatic collapse. KKR went from 115/5 to being bowled out for 176, losing 5 wickets for just 10 runs in a breathtaking implosion.

The chase was anchored by a sublime 83* from **Priyansh Arya**, who combined with Atharva Taide (38) and Rajat Patidar (42*) to keep RCB ahead of the rate. Despite losing Gayle early and Taide at the halfway mark, Arya and Patidar took the game away from KKR with a flurry of boundaries, sealing a comfortable 8-wicket victory in the 18th over.
