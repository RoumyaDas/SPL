SPL Season 5 | Match 87: GT vs DC at GT

DC won the toss and chose to bowl first. GT got off to a flying start thanks to a brilliant unbeaten 96 from just 57 balls by Ruturaj Gaikwad, who anchored the innings beautifully. Despite his knock and a late cameo from Glenn Phillips (30 off 12), GT lost wickets in clusters towards the end, managing 188/6 in 20 overs. For DC, Sandeep Lamichhane (3/19) was exceptional with the ball, supported by Duan Ferreira (2/26).

In reply, DC's chase was powered by a stunning 87 off just 41 balls from MJ Owen, who kept his team in the hunt despite regular wickets. A crucial 52 off 22 from Tarang Gohel accelerated the innings in the middle overs. DC lost 9 wickets but chased down the target of 189 with 9 balls to spare, winning a thrilling contest. George Linde (3/32) was the pick of GT's bowlers.
