**🏏 SPL S07, Match 1: RCB vs MI – A Patidar Masterclass Crushes MI**

The season opener turned into a one-man demolition show as RM Patidar announced his arrival in S07 with a jaw-dropping, unbeaten 104 off just 39 balls. After MI opted to bowl first on a pace-friendly pitch, RCB’s openers started aggressively, but it was Patidar’s entry that changed the game entirely. Walking in at 94/3, he tore into every bowler — smashing 12 fours and 6 sixes at a strike rate of 266.67. His carnage powered RCB to a mammoth 262/6, leaving MI shell-shocked.

Chasing 263, MI’s chase never took off. Abhishek Sharma fought hard with a fluent 53, but RCB’s spin duo of PWH de Silva (3/23) and SS Ghosh (3/35) ripped through the middle order. MI kept losing wickets at regular intervals and folded for just 148 in 19 overs. RCB won by 114 runs, and Patidar walked away with a well-deserved Player of the Match award.
