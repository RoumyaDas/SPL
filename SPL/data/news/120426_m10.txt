📝 **Match Summary – SPL S07, Match 10: CSK vs SRH**

Toss: SRH opted to bowl first on a balanced pitch.

CSK’s innings was a two-man rescue act. After early blows – Gaikwad (7), Tilak (11), and ABD (1) fell cheaply – **B Sai Sudharsan** (81* off 49) and **KK Nair** (65 off 37) stitched a magnificent 123-run stand. Nair smashed three sixes in the death before falling, while Sudharsan remained unbeaten. **NT Ellis** (3/40) led SRH’s attack, but CSK reached a solid 180/6.

Chasing 181, SRH lost Short (25) early, but **P Simran Singh** (67* off 49) held one end together. Then came **VR Iyer** – a brutal 67 off just 22 balls (7 sixes, 3 fours). He walked in at 97/3 in the 13th over and absolutely demolished CSK’s bowlers, hitting sixes at will. A 24-run over off Chahal turned the chase on its head. SRH galloped home with 10 balls to spare.

A heist powered by Iyer’s hurricane.
