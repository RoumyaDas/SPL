🏏 **SPL S07 Match 40: PBKS vs RR – Spin Web Spells Doom for PBKS**

Toss went PBKS’s way, but the pitch was a rank-turner (spin assistance 4x), and they walked straight into RR’s trap. PBKS’s innings never got off the blocks—openers fell cheaply, and a catastrophic collapse saw them at 17/4 inside 3 overs. V Suryavanshi (31 off 23) tried to rebuild, but wickets kept tumbling. **Mohammad Ghazanfar** ran riot with a sensational 4/8 in 2 overs, while **Karan Lal** mopped up the tail with 3/0. PBKS were skittled for just 75 in 12.2 overs—their lowest of the season.

Chasing a paltry 76, RR lost Jaiswal (15) and Rickelton early, but **S Dube** stood firm with an unbeaten 34 off 25, anchoring the chase. Aniket Verma chipped in with a brisk 19, and RR comfortably cruised to 76/4 in 12.2 overs. PBKS bowlers tried hard—Prashant Veer, Utkarsh Singh, CV Varun, and MP Yadav each took a wicket—but the target was too small.

A crushing win for RR, powered by Ghazanfar’s magical spell. PBKS collapse was the real story here.
