SPL Season 5 | Match 90: MI vs KKR at MI

KKR won the toss and chose to bowl first. MI started aggressively but lost wickets regularly. MW Short smashed a quick 50 off just 19 balls, and SR Hain contributed 49, but the rest of the lineup collapsed against a disciplined KKR attack. Mitchell Starc (3/21) and Bhuvneshwar Kumar (3/36) were outstanding, bowling out MI for 164 in 19.3 overs.

In reply, KKR lost MP Stoinis early, but SP Narine (24) and RM Patidar (20) kept the scoreboard ticking. The chase was completely dominated by Cameron Green, who blasted an unbeaten 69 off only 28 balls, with RG Sharma adding 29. Despite a late double-strike from SS Ghosh (3/21), KKR comfortably chased down the target with 24 balls to spare, winning by 5 wickets.
