# 🏏 SPL S08 – Match 37: MI vs KKR – A Chase for the Ages

MI won the toss and elected to bowl first on a spin-friendly surface. KKR's innings was a rollercoaster ride. After losing Ishan Kishan early, WL Madsen anchored the innings with a well-compiled 52, but it was Jay Gohil's explosive 58 off just 23 balls in the death overs that propelled KKR to a formidable 187. MI's bowlers shared the wickets around, with Ehsan Khan and Shivam Shukla picking up two each, but they couldn't contain Gohil's late assault.

Chasing 188, MI openers Tushar Raheja and Urvil Patel came out with intent, putting on a rapid 63-run stand inside the powerplay. Raheja fell early, but Patel continued his sublime form, smashing a breathtaking 83 off 44 balls. Abhishek Sharma and Kunal Chandela provided solid support, and despite a late wobble, MR Marsh and Siddhesh Veer finished the chase with a flurry of boundaries, sealing the victory with more than three overs to spare. A clinical chase that showcased MI's batting depth and composure under pressure.
