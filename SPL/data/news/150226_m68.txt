🏏 **Match 68: GT vs DC – Summary**

DC won the toss and opted to field first on a balanced pitch at GT. Openers JP Inglis (21) and SV Samson (17) fell early, but AA Kulkarni (69 off 37) and Kumar Kushagra (49 off 38) resurrected the innings with a crucial 80-run partnership. SA Yadav (36 off 18) continued the assault before Sanvir Singh triggered a collapse with two wickets in an over. However, N Sindhu (32 off 16) and MJ Owen (18 off 10) kept the chase on track, taking DC to the brink.
