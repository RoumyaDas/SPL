# 🏏 SPL S07 Match 13: PBKS vs GT – Spin Web & Dhir's Lone Battle

GT opted to bat first on a spin-friendly pitch (2x assistance). Disaster struck immediately as Samson fell first ball for a golden duck. But Naman Dhir (82 off 50, 5 fours, 3 sixes) anchored magnificently, stitching partnerships with Inglis (40) and Parag (23). A late cameo from Nigam (18* off 6) propelled GT to 192/5. PBKS spinners Ramjani (1/37) and Narine (1/19) bowled tight lines.

Chasing 193, PBKS lost Narine (0) and Rawat (1) early. Suryavanshi (63 off 38) and Ura (67 off 32) launched a fierce counterattack, adding 94 runs. At 141/3 in the 13th over, PBKS needed just 52 from 42 balls with 7 wickets in hand — the game was theirs to lose. Then came Sufiyan Muqeem (3/34) and R Parag (1/0 off 6 balls!). The spin choke tightened. PBKS lost their last 7 wickets for just 41 runs, crumbling to 182 all out. GT won by 10 runs in a stunning collapse.

**Winner:** GT  
**Player of the Match:** Naman Dhir (82 off 50)
