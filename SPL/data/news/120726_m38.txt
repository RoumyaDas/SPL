# 🏏 SPL S08 – Match 38: RCB vs DC – A Bowling Masterclass

DC won the toss and elected to bowl first on a pace-friendly surface, and what a decision it turned out to be. Easwaran K, playing only his second match, produced a sensational spell, removing both RCB openers for ducks in the very first over. RCB's innings never recovered from that double blow, as they kept losing wickets at regular intervals. Priyansh Arya (40) and RM Patidar (35) tried to rebuild, but DC's bowlers were relentless. Easwaran K finished with extraordinary figures of 4/33, while AD Russell and JJ Bumrah chipped in with two wickets each, bowling with incredible discipline. RCB were bundled out for just 126 in 15.2 overs, their innings crumbling under pressure.

Chasing a modest 127, DC openers GJ Maxwell and MJ Owen came out with positive intent. Maxwell was in rampant form, smashing 49 off just 22 balls, while SA Yadav played a supporting role with a fluent 49. The pair put on a 77-run partnership, virtually ending the contest. RCB's bowlers had no answers to the onslaught, as DC cruised to the target in just 11.3 overs, losing only three wickets. A comprehensive victory for DC, powered by a brilliant bowling display and a clinical chase.
