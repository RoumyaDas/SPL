**Match S07M030: MI vs SRH – Spin Web Strangles MI; SRH Cruise**

On a spin-friendly Wankhede (1.8x spin assistance), MI elected to bat first – and promptly imploded. SRH’s spin trio of Zampa (3/17), Sai Kishore (3/24), and Shadab Khan (2/45) ran through MI’s batting. After losing four wickets inside the PowerPlay, only Smaran Ravichandran (45 off 29) and AJ Hosein (30* off 20) offered resistance. MI folded for 144 – a total that never threatened.

Chasing 145, SRH openers P Simran Singh (54 off 36) and Sahil Parakh (38 off 30) put on 87 for the first wicket, killing the chase in the PowerPlay itself. KL Rahul (34* off 23) finished it with calm authority. SRH lost only three wickets and romped home with 23 balls to spare. A clinical, spin-dominated demolition.
