SRH won the toss and elected to bat first at their home venue. They got off to a shaky start, losing early wickets including openers P Simran Singh and Priyansh Arya. KL Rahul anchored the innings with a solid 60* off 44 balls, while Nithish Kumar Reddy (36 off 27) and Shadab Khan (44* off 21) provided the late fireworks to propel SRH to a competitive 177/6 in 20 overs. For GT, GD Phillips and Shahbaz Ahmed took 2 wickets each.

In reply, GT's chase never gained momentum as they lost wickets at regular intervals. SV Samson (29 off 22) and R Parag (23 off 14) showed some resistance, but SRH's bowlers dominated. Shadab Khan was exceptional with 3/25, well-supported by KA Maharaj (3/35) and Mohammad Ghazanfar (2/29). GT collapsed to 132 all out in 17.4 overs, handing SRH a comprehensive 45-run victory.
