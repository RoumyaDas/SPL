# 🏏 SPL S07 Match 18: CSK vs DC – A Kotian Masterclass & A Ruthless Chase

DC won the toss and elected to bat first on a balanced pitch. Disaster struck early – **GJ Maxwell** (1) holed out in the first over. But **SA Yadav** (68 off 30) launched a stunning counterattack, smashing 11 fours and 2 sixes. Alongside **Tarang Gohel** (39 off 31), DC looked set for a massive total. Then **Tanush Kotian** happened. The off-spinner ripped through the middle order – **SA Yadav**, **Markram**, **Karan Shinde** – all gone in a dizzying collapse. Kotian finished with a sensational **5-wicket haul** (5/38). **Geet Puri** (3/17) mopped up the tail. DC crumbled from 96/1 to **161 all out** – losing 9 wickets for just 65 runs.

Chasing 162, CSK openers **B Sai Sudharsan** (65* off 52) and **Tilak Varma** (52 off 27) were relentless. Tilak attacked from ball one, reaching his fifty in style. The pair added 88 runs before Tilak fell, but the damage was done. **Karun Nair** (39* off 25) joined Sudharsan and the two cruised to victory. CSK chased down the target in **17.1 overs** with 9 wickets in hand. A statement win. DC's batting implosion and Kotian's magic turned the game into a one-sided affair.
