⚔️ **SPL S07, Match 61: GT vs CSK – A Five-Wicket Haul in Vain**

GT opted to bat first on a balanced surface. **SV Samson** blazed 39 off 18, and **Naman Dhir** anchored with an unbeaten 58. But CSK’s **Geet Puri** ran through the lower order, claiming a stunning **5-wicket haul**, restricting GT to **196/9**.

Chasing 197, CSK’s innings was a rollercoaster. **KK Nair** played a brilliant hand of 46 off 25, but wickets fell at regular intervals. With 10 runs needed off the final over, CSK had two wickets in hand. But **GF Linde** held his nerve, conceding just 8 runs and picking up the big wicket of Nair. CSK fell agonizingly **1 run short** in a heart-stopping finish. GT clinched a thriller by 1 run.
