📝 **SPL S07 Match 22: GT vs SRH – Ellis Magic, but Reddy-Linde Hold Nerve**

On a spin-friendly surface, SRH batted first and recovered from early blows through Kartik Sharma (51 off 27) and Venkatesh Iyer (50* off 35). Hardik Pandya's 33 pushed them to 192/7. GT's spinners – Linde (2/21), Muqeem (2/26) – bowled tight lines.

In reply, GT openers Samson (38 off 21) and Inglis (55 off 34) added 89 in just 47 balls, setting the platform. But Nathan Ellis produced a stunning spell of 4/27, triggering a collapse. GT slipped to 121/5, still needing 72 from 48.

Enter Nitish Kumar Reddy (30* off 17) and George Linde (23* off 17). The duo stitched an unbroken 52-run stand off 33 balls, calmly guiding GT home with 8 deliveries to spare. Ellis was unplayable, but Reddy and Linde held their nerve in a tense chase. GT wins by 4 wickets. 🏏💥
