# 🏏 Match 48 – CSK vs RCB: A High-Scoring Thriller at Chepauk! ⚡

CSK opted to bat first on a pitch tilted towards pacers. They had a steady start, but regular wickets kept them in check. The middle order stuttered, and it was a late flourish from Tanush Kotian (45) and Sonu Yadav (33*) that propelled CSK to a competitive total of 193. M Pathirana and HJ Manenti were the pick of the RCB bowlers, sharing 4 wickets between them.

In reply, RCB openers Atharva Taide and Priyansh Arya came out all guns blazing. They tore into the CSK bowling, putting on a 91-run partnership. Priyansh Arya smashed a breathtaking 60, while Taide contributed a solid 42. Despite a few hiccups in the middle, RCB kept their composure. A late cameo from Akash Pawar (22) and a massive six from PWH de Silva sealed the chase with 10 balls to spare. It was a dominant batting display from RCB.
