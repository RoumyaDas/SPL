📋 **Match 34 – DC vs CSK (Venue: DC)**

CSK won the toss and elected to bowl first on a pace-friendly pitch. DC's innings was powered by a sensational unbeaten 86 off just 40 balls from N Sindhu, who single-handedly dragged them to 201/6. Geet Puri was the pick of CSK bowlers with a brilliant 3-wicket haul, but DC's total looked competitive.

The chase began steadily, but DC's bowlers struck early to keep things tight. At 131/4, the game was in the balance. Then came Tanush Kotian — walked in at No. 7 and absolutely demolished the death overs. He smashed an unreal 32* off just 8 balls (5 fours, 2 sixes), including a 26-run over off Shaheen Afridi that sealed the game. CSK chased down the target with 2 overs to spare, winning by 5 wickets. Absolute carnage. 🧨🔥

**Player of the Match:** N Sindhu (DC)
