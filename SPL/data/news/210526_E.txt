🏆 SPL S07 – ELIMINATOR | GT vs DC | MI Ground (Spin Paradise - 3x)

TOSS: DC elected to BAT first on a pitch tailor-made for spinners.

FIRST INNINGS – THE MAXWELL STORM & LINDE'S FIGHTBACK

DC openers walked into a spin trap. Tarang Gohel fell for a golden duck – caught by Samson off Linde's very first over. Karan Shinde (9 off 7) followed soon after, caught by Naman Dhir off Linde again. GJ Maxwell, however, was in a different zone. He smashed 32 off 16 (6 fours) before J Overton cut his innings short. Enter N Sindhu. The DC star launched a brutal assault – 47 off 19 (10 fours), including 20 runs off a single D Brevis over. But GT hit back. Overton removed Sindhu (caught by Rishabh Chouhan). Sufiyan Muqeem and V Nigam chipped away at the middle order. AK Markram (25 off 16) and Parth Chandan (24 off 16) tried to rebuild, but Linde returned to clean up Chandan. DC were bowled out for 163 in 17.1 overs. GF Linde (3/17 in 2.1 overs) and J Overton (3/25 in 4 overs) starred.

SECOND INNINGS – THE NAMAN DHIR MASTERCLASS

GT needed 164. SV Samson fell early – bowled by Harsh Dubey for 9. JP Inglis (48 off 29) and R Parag (20 off 15) added stability. Parag fell to Bumrah. Enter Naman Dhir. He walked in at 54/2 and took control. Inglis fell to Markram for 48, but Dhir found an ally in D Brevis (5 off 7). Dhir reached his fifty – 51 off 29 (3 fours, 2 sixes) – before falling to Markram. At 139/5, GT needed 25. Rishabh Chouhan walked in. He smashed 18* off 7 (1 six), partnered by GF Linde (5 off 3). Chouhan finished the chase with a towering six off AK Markram in the 19th over. GT won by 3 wickets with 11 balls to spare. AK Markram fought hard with 3/35, but it wasn't enough.

GT march to Qualifier 2. DC eliminated.
