# 🏏 SPL S07 Match 17: KKR vs PBKS – A Pace Paradise & A Collapse to Forget

KKR won the toss and chose to bat first on a pitch promising 4x more help for pace than spin. It started sedately, but **Rohan Kunnummal** (37 off 21) and **Ben Duckett** (18 off 15) laid a decent foundation. Then the wheels fell off. **Cameron Green** fell for a golden duck – run out without facing a ball. **Rohit Sharma** followed for 1 – another run out. **D Wiese**? Golden duck. **MA Starc**? Another duck. **Bhuvneshwar Kumar**? Yet another duck. KKR's middle-lower order was obliterated by **JJ Smit** (3/30) and **Manvanth Kumar L** (2/11). Only **Suryansh Shedge** (23* off 18) offered a flicker of defiance. KKR folded for a measly **134 all out** in 17.4 overs. Seven batters failed to reach double digits. Embarrassing.

Chasing 135, PBKS lost **V Suryavanshi** first ball – LBW to B Kumar. A nightmare start. But **Rilee Rossouw** (57 off 40) and **Nitish Rana** (36 off 28) rebuilt with calm precision. They rotated strike, punished bad balls, and kept the required rate under control. Rossouw fell to Ramandeep Singh after a composed fifty, but the chase was already in the bag. **Tristan Stubbs** (21* off 11) finished with a flourish. PBKS chased down the target in **15.4 overs** with 6 wickets in hand. A professional, no-nonsense chase. KKR's batting implosion will haunt them.
