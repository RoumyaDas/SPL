📋 **Match 33 – MI vs PBKS (Venue: MI)**

MI won the toss and elected to bowl first on a spin-friendly surface. PBKS opener V Suryavanshi played the anchor role to perfection, carrying his bat through the innings for an unbeaten 93 off 52 balls. He found solid support from Narine (46) and a late cameo from Ura (25*), pushing PBKS to a competitive 191/3. MI's bowlers toiled hard but couldn't break through consistently — Noor Ahmad was economical but wicketless, while Shami leaked runs.

The chase turned into a nightmare for MI. Left-arm spinner AR Ramjani produced a spell for the ages — 3/14 in 4 overs, strangling the batting lineup with 15 dot balls. MI lost wickets in clusters: Abhishek Sharma fell in the first over, and the middle order never recovered. Smaran Ravichandran fought a lonely battle with 63* off 39 balls, but he ran out of partners as MI limped to 148/8 — falling 43 runs short. A complete bowling masterclass from PBKS. 🧨🕸️

**Player of the Match:** V Suryavanshi (PBKS)
