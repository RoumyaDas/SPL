🏏 **SPL S07 Match 36: CSK vs RCB – Taide and Gayle Crush CSK on a Spinning Track**

CSK opted to bat first on a spin-friendly pitch (3.3x assistance). **Heinrich Klaasen** (67 off 46) anchored the innings, while **Karun Nair** (49* off 32) provided late impetus, helping CSK reach 187/8. RCB's spin trio—**Imran Tahir** (2/28), **Abrar Ahmed** (2/31), and **Wanindu Hasaranga** (2/32)—kept things tight throughout.

Chasing 188, RCB openers **Chris Gayle** (54 off 29) and **Atharva Taide** (80 off 46) put on a blistering 101-run stand. After Gayle fell, Taide continued the assault before departing for 80. **Salil Arora** (26* off 11) and **Hasaranga** (11* off 5) finished the chase with 3.2 overs to spare. RCB won by 7 wickets in a dominant run chase. CSK's spinners were taken to the cleaners—Tanay Thyagarajan went for 58 in 4 overs.
