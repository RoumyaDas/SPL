**Match S07M026: PBKS Snatch Thriller from DC’s Grasp in a Low-Scoring Nail-Biter**

Toss: PBKS opted to bat first on a pace-friendly track in Delhi. Their openers started aggressively, but an early LBW to Gurnoor Brar brought in N Rana, who looked in sublime touch, smashing 35 off just 18 balls. However, DC’s bowlers struck at regular intervals. Shaheen Afridi and Bumrah delivered devastating blows in the death, triggering a collapse. A late cameo from JJ Smit (16 off 4) pushed PBKS to 198, but being bowled out with 10 balls unused suggested they’d left runs on the table.

Chasing 199, DC’s star batter GJ Maxwell exploded out of the blocks, reaching 41 off 20. But just when the chase looked settled, AR Ramjani struck twice in the PowerPlay, removing Maxwell and then SA Yadav. The middle order struggled to accelerate on a slowing surface. Despite Tarang Gohel’s defiant 36* off 18, DC kept losing wickets. JJ Smit (3/44) and Ramjani (3/23) held their nerve in the death, defending 15 off the final over. PBKS won by 14 runs, with DC finishing 184/9 — a classic case of falling just short after a fiery start.
