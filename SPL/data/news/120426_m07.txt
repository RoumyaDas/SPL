📝 **Match Summary – SPL S07, Match 7: DC vs MI**

Toss: DC opted to bat first on a pace-friendly deck (2.2x pace assistance).

DC’s innings was a fireworks show. **GJ Maxwell** (54 off 23) blitzed 11 fours and a six, racing to his fifty before falling. Tarang Gohel (36) provided steady support, but it was **Karan Shinde** (34* off 13) who finished with a fury – 3 fours, 3 sixes in a late onslaught. MI’s **MR Marsh** (3/51) fought back, but DC posted a massive 207/6.

Chasing 208, MI started aggressively – Abhishek Sharma (26 off 10) and Urvil Patel (31 off 15) flew out of the blocks. Then came **JJ Bumrah**. The nightmare began: 6/29 in 3.5 overs. He ripped through the middle and lower order – Iyer (53) fought alone, but wickets tumbled in a heap. MI collapsed from 132/3 to 185 all out.

A brutal bowling masterclass sealed a 22-run win for DC.
