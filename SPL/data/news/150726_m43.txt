# 🏏 SPL S08 - Match 43: LSG vs SRH

**Toss:** SRH won the toss and elected to bat first on a pitch offering significant pace assistance.

The first innings was a masterclass in calculated aggression, anchored by a phenomenal century from SRH’s **KL Rahul**. He weathered the early storm after **MP Stoinis** was dismissed in the first over, forming a crucial partnership with the explosive **Kartik Sharma**. After Sharma’s dismissal for a brisk 43, the stage was set for the death overs. **VR Iyer** came in like a force of nature, dismantling the LSG bowling attack with audacious strokeplay, and, alongside Rahul's brilliant unbeaten 100, propelled SRH to a massive total.

In response, LSG's chase started with a flurry of boundaries from **Shubman Gill**. However, his dismissal triggered a collapse, and despite a fighting half-century from **Ajay Rohera**, the required run rate kept climbing. When Rohera was dismissed for a well-made 72, the chase was effectively over. A late cameo from **Shubhang Hegde** was merely a consolation, as SRH's bowlers, led by the economical **NT Ellis**, held their nerve perfectly to snatch victory in a thrilling finish.

SRH wins by 7 runs, defending the total successfully!
