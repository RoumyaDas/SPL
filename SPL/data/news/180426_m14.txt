# 🏏 SPL S07 Match 14: CSK vs LSG – Spin Carnage & Gill's Masterclass

On a pitch offering 4x spin assistance, CSK opted to bat first. Sudharsan (20) and Tilak (14) started brightly, but Kuldeep Yadav (4/36) spun a web, removing both openers and later Klaasen (36) and AB de Villiers (29). Gaikwad (42) fought hard but found no support. CSK collapsed from 110/3 to 171/9, with Arshdeep (3/39) and Ssenyondo (2/26) tightening the screws. Spinners took 8 of the 9 wickets.

Chasing 172, Shubman Gill (79 off 41, 11 fours, 2 sixes) launched a breathtaking assault. He lost partners early but kept the scoreboard racing. Mousley (26 off 14) and Jacks (18) chipped in, but the chase stuttered briefly. Then Sameer Rizvi (31* off 13) walked in and finished it with surgical precision — hitting four boundaries in a calm, unbeaten knock. LSG chased down the target in 16.2 overs, winning by 5 wickets. The spinners who choked CSK couldn't contain LSG's aggressive batting.

**Winner:** LSG  
**Player of the Match:** Kuldeep Yadav (4/36)
