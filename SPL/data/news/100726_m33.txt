# 🏏 SPL S08 Match 33 – MI vs PBKS: Patel Anchors a Chase for the Ages

The pace-friendly strip in Mumbai saw PBKS elect to bat first, and they came out swinging. **V Suryavanshi** and **TL Seifert** got PBKS off to a blazing start, with Suryavanshi smashing 14 off 7 before falling to **Sikandar Raza**. PBKS then lost **N Rana** cheaply, but **MS Pepper** took center stage with a fluent 64 off 40 balls, while **Shashank Singh** played the perfect foil with an unbeaten 63 off 32 deliveries. **JJ Smit** chipped in with a cameo of 17, and PBKS posted a competitive 204/6.

Chasing 205, MI lost **Tushar Raheja** early to a brilliant run-out, but **Urvil Patel** had other plans. He anchored the chase with a masterclass of composure and aggression, scoring an unbeaten 94 off 57 balls. **Abhishek Sharma** provided the early fireworks with a quickfire 38, and **MR Marsh** finished the game in style with an unbeaten 20 off just 6 balls, including two massive sixes and a four to seal the victory with an over to spare.

A clinical chase orchestrated by the brilliant Urvil Patel, who walked away with the Player of the Match for his near-century knock.

**MI wins by 7 wickets (with 6 balls to spare)!**
