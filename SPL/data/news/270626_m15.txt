# 🏏 Match 15 – DC vs PBKS: A Run-Fest for the Ages

In a match that will be remembered for its sheer brutality with the bat, PBKS pulled off a record-breaking chase against DC in a high-octane encounter. Batting first, DC unleashed an onslaught, with Glenn Maxwell playing a breathtaking knock of 80, while Tarang Gohel and Suryakumar Yadav chipped in with blazing half-centuries. The trio propelled DC to a mammoth 235, a total that seemed insurmountable on a pitch that was beginning to take turn.

However, PBKS had other plans. In a chase for the ages, they came out with an aggressive mindset, with Vaibhav Suryavanshi and Tim Seifert providing a flying start. But it was the young sensation R Smaran who stole the show, playing the innings of his life. He anchored the chase with a sensational 86, combining classical strokeplay with brutal power-hitting. Despite losing wickets at regular intervals, PBKS kept the required rate in check. The equation came down to 4 runs off the final over, and Shashank Singh smashed a six off the very first ball to seal a historic victory for PBKS. A remarkable win that showcased the relentless spirit of the PBKS batting line-up.
