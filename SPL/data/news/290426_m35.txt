📋 **Match 35 – RR vs LSG (Venue: RR)**

RR won the toss and elected to bat first on a pitch tailor-made for spin. The openers came out with intent, but the real story was the complete decimation of the LSG bowling attack. Jaiswal and Rickelton added 137 for the first wicket, treating pace and spin with equal disdain. After Jaiswal fell for a blistering 68, Rickelton carried on to 72 before being trapped LBW. Then came the final flourish — S Dube walked in and smashed an unbeaten 55 off just 23 balls, launching four sixes as RR piled up a mammoth 219. LSG’s bowlers had no answers; their most economical bowler went for over 9 an over.

In response, LSG lost Mousley early, but what followed was a chase for the ages. Shubman Gill played perhaps the innings of the season — unbeaten on 119 off 58 balls, a masterclass in pacing a chase. Despite wickets tumbling at the other end, Gill kept finding boundaries at will. With 17 needed off the last over, the required rate soared, but Gill almost single-handedly dragged LSG to the brink. In the end, they fell just 1 run short of one of the greatest heists — finishing at 218/5. Absolute heartbreak. RR held their nerve by a single run. 🏏🔥
