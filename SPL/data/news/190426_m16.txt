# 🏏 SPL S07 Match 16: GT vs MI – A Spin Web & A Chase Too Far

Toss went GT's way, and they opted to bowl first on a pitch that promised to assist spin 4x more than pace. MI's openers, however, came out with a different plan. **Abhishek Sharma** (33 off 15) threw the first punch, smashing five boundaries and a six in a fiery PowerPlay. But just as MI were motoring at 50/0, **Washington Sundar** struck – Abhishek holed out.

**Urvil Patel** (40 off 30) and **Tushar Raheja** (40 off 20) kept the scoreboard ticking, but wickets began to tumble in clusters. **Sufiyan Muqeem** and **GF Linde** spun a tightening web. Linde, in particular, was relentless – picking up 4 crucial wickets, including a stunner of a stumping and a Hit-Wicket off the last ball of the innings. MI's middle order tried to accelerate, but **N Pooran**'s run-out for a brisk 40 derailed the tail. MI limped to **210/9** – a fighting total, but perhaps 15-20 runs short on this surface.

Chasing 211, GT's **Sanju Samson** (50 off 20) exploded like a man on a mission. He tore into Ravi Teja with three sixes and a four in an over, racing to his fifty in no time. **JP Inglis** (9) fell early, but **Naman Dhir** (42 off 27) and **Riyan Parag** (61 off 33) stitched a calm yet calculated stand. Parag was surgical – picking his spots, clearing the ropes five times.

Then came the twist. Two quick LBWs – Dhir and then Nithish Reddy – brought the equation down to 38 off 18. Enter **Rishabh Chouhan** (30* off 12). Uncapped, unbothered. He muscled fours and sixes at will. With 3 needed off the final over, he finished it in one ball – a massive six. GT chased down **214/7** with 5 balls to spare. **GF Linde** was named Player of the Match for his all-round brilliance, but the night belonged to GT's fearless chase.
