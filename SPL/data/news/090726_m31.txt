# 🏏 SPL S08 Match 31 – KKR vs LSG: A Run-Fest Decided by the Thinnest of Margins

The pitch in Kolkata promised spin, but the batters turned it into a nightmare for the bowlers. LSG won the toss and elected to bat first on a track that offered little to no assistance, and **Shubman Gill** made them pay immediately.

Opening the innings, **Shubman Gill** looked like a man possessed. He was rock-solid yet aggressively elegant, bringing up his century off just 58 balls. He lost his opening partner **FH Allen** early for a duck, and **DP Conway** fell for a quick 5, but **Abishek Porel** joined Gill to steady the ship with a composed 61 off 39 balls. Then came the carnage—**Sameer Rizvi** walked in and smashed 44 off just 10 balls at a mind-boggling strike rate of 440, propelling LSG to a massive 224/3. Gill remained unbeaten on 108, showcasing why he is one of the most feared batters in the league.

The chase began with **Virat Singh** coming out of nowhere, smashing 40 off 25 balls and providing the spark alongside **Ishan Kishan**. Kishan, in sensational touch, played one of the best knocks of the tournament, smashing an unbeaten 116 off 61 balls with 15 fours and 2 towering sixes. **WL Madsen** joined the party with a fluent 56 off 31 balls, keeping KKR in the hunt. With 19 needed off the last over, Madsen fell, and **C Green** walked in to face a solitary ball before **SS Iyer** smashed a six and a two to bring KKR to 223/3. But it wasn't enough—they fell short by just 1 run in a classic thriller.

A 1-run win in a 224-run chase — absolute mayhem, and **Ishan Kishan** walked away with the Player of the Match for his majestic hundred.

**LSG wins by 1 run!**
