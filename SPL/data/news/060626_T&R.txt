# S08 Mini Auction Preview: Franchises Announce Major Releases, Trades Reshape Lineups

Ahead of the S08 Mini Auction, all ten franchises have submitted their official release lists. A total of 60 players have been let go, freeing up significant salary cap space. Additionally, ten pre-negotiated trades have dramatically altered team rosters even before the auction begins.

## Released Players: Big Names Back in the Pool

Several high-profile players will now enter the auction pool after being released by their franchises.

### Top Releases by Value

- **CSK** has released **Yuzvendra Chahal** (₹11.75 crore) and **AB de Villiers** (₹12 crore), sending two of their biggest match-winners back into the auction.
- **LSG** let go of **Jofra Archer** (₹8.25 crore) and **Shane Watson** (₹12 crore), a surprising move given their impact potential.
- **KKR** parted ways with **Ravi Singh** (₹4 crore), **Ramandeep Singh** (₹1 crore), **Kumar Kartikeya Singh** (₹0.6 crore), **MS Dhoni** (₹0.8 crore), **Liam Livingstone** (₹5.5 crore), and several others.
- **SRH** released **R. Sai Kishore** (₹8.25 crore), **Abdul Samad** (₹4 crore), **Vignesh Puthur** (₹4 crore), **Matthew Short** (₹3.5 crore), and **Shadab Khan** (₹1.3 crore), among others.
- **RCB** made headlines by releasing uncapped **Akshat Raghuwanshi** (₹8.5 crore), along with **Akshya Deka** (₹2.5 crore) and several low-cost players.
- **MI** let go of **Jagjit Singh** (₹1.3 crore), **Khrievitso Kense** (₹5.25 crore), **James Coles** (₹0.6 crore), **Mayank Agarwal** (₹0.5 crore), **Shahrukh Khan** (₹7.25 crore), and **Abhinav Manohar** (₹4 crore).
- **PBKS** released **Tristan Stubbs** (₹7.5 crore) and **Suyash Prabhudessai** (₹0.3 crore).
- **RR** released **Anmolpreet Singh** (₹6.25 crore), **Mohammad Nabi** (₹1.3 crore), **Anukul Roy** (₹0.3 crore), **Dinesh Karthik** (₹0.5 crore), and **Ben Stokes** (₹0.5 crore).
- **DC** released **Brandon McMullen** (₹4.75 crore), **Akash Vasisht** (₹2.75 crore), and **Dan Lawrence** (₹0.5 crore).
- **GT** released **Jacob Bethell** (₹0.9 crore), **Arshin Kulkarni** (₹2 crore), **Ottneil Baartman** (₹0.5 crore), and **Auqib Dar** (₹1.6 crore).

## Trade Window: Kohli to CSK, Rohit to RCB in Landmark Deals

Ten trades were executed prior to the auction, resulting in major player movement.

### Trade 6: Kohli-Pollard Swap

**Virat Kohli** (₹16 crore) moves from **RR** to **CSK**, while **Kieron Pollard** (₹13.5 crore) heads to **RR**. This trade fundamentally alters the batting cores of both teams.

### Trade 8: Cornerstone Trade – Rohit Sharma to RCB

**Rohit Sharma** (₹14 crore) joins **RCB** from **KKR**, with **Salil Arora** (₹6.5 crore) moving to KKR. RCB secures a marquee captaincy candidate.

### Trade 7: Hardik Pandya to LSG

In a suprise deal, **Hardik Pandya** (₹14.25 crore) goes from **SRH** to **LSG**, **Will Jacks** (₹13 crore) moves to **SRH**, and **Mayank Markande** (₹1.6 crore) also joins SRH from LSG.

### Other Notable Trades

- **Trade 1:** **Sakibul Gani** (₹8 crore) to RR; **Naseem Shah** (₹0.5 crore) to MI.
- **Trade 4:** **Shreyas Iyer** (₹5.25 crore) to KKR; **Rohan Kunnummal** (₹4 crore) to MI.
- **Trade 5:** **Jos Buttler** (₹6 crore) to DC; **Karan Shinde** (₹6.75 crore) and **Krunal Pandya** (₹0.5 crore) to SRH.
- **Trade 9:** **Sandeep Lamichhane** (₹2 crore) to RCB; **Spencer Johnson** (₹4.25 crore) and **Abrar Ahmed** (₹0.5 crore) to KKR.

## Auction Outlook

With a deep pool of released talent including Chahal, Archer, Livingstone, and Dhoni now available, the S08 Mini Auction promises intense bidding wars. Franchises have cleared significant salary cap space, and the trades have set the stage for a dramatic rebuild across multiple teams.
