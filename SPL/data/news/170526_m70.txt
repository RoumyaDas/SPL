# 🏏 SPL S07 Match 70: PBKS vs KKR – Smit and Starc Sink PBKS on a Pace-Friendly Track

KKR opted to bat first on a surface tailor-made for fast bowling. Early blows from JJ Smit (4/34) and CV Varun (3/28) reduced KKR to a precarious 22/3 inside the powerplay. But then Rohit Sharma and Cam Green staged a magnificent rescue act. The duo added over 100 runs, with Rohit anchoring on 62 and Green playing the perfect foil with 59. Late cameos from Rawat (22 off 7) and Shedge (17 off 9) pushed KKR to a competitive 184/7.

In reply, PBKS came out firing. Seifert blazed 40 off 17, and Suryavanshi chipped in with 23. But the introduction of B Kumar and Mitchell Starc turned the game on its head. Starc (3/9 in 2 overs) ripped through the middle order in a breathtaking spell. B Kumar (4/31) then mopped up the tail as PBKS slumped from 60/1 to 132 all out inside 16 overs. A crushing 52-run defeat for PBKS, with KKR's pace pack running riot on home turf.

**Winner:** KKR  
**Player of the Match:** JJ Smit (KKR) – for his game-changing 4/34.
