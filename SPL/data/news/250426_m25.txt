📝 **SPL S07 Match 25: KKR vs RR – Rickelton's Lone War Falls Short in a Pace-Fest**

On a pitch offering monstrous assistance to pace (4x!), KKR opted to bat first. RR's bowlers, led by Cheepurapalli Stephen (2/28), kept things relatively tight, but KKR's depth proved too much. Ishan Kishan anchored with a steady 54 off 38, while RG Sharma (31 off 13) and D Wiese (29* off 13) provided late fireworks to push KKR to a formidable 202/5.

Chasing, RR lost Virat Kohli for a golden duck in the second ball – a massive blow. But Ryan Rickelton stood like a rock, crafting a magnificent 71 off 46. He found support from S Dube (38 off 30), but the asking rate kept climbing. Once Rickelton fell LBW to Pulkit Narang in the 17th over, RR's hopes faded. YBK Jaiswal smashed a fiery 46* off 18, but it was too little, too late. RR finished at 191/4, falling 11 runs short.

Rickelton's heroic 71 went in vain as KKR's total proved just out of reach. A thrilling contest where every run counted. 🏏⚡
