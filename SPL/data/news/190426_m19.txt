# 🏏 SPL S07 Match 19: SRH vs LSG – Gill's Grit, Arshdeep's Ice

LSG won the toss and opted to bat first on a balanced pitch. **Shubman Gill** (73 off 43) and **Finn Allen** (25 off 17) gave them a flying start. After Allen fell, **Will Jacks** (52 off 36) joined Gill in a steady 80-run stand. Gill fell in the 17th over, but **Abishek Porel** (37* off 12) and **Sameer Rizvi** (9* off 3) unleashed carnage in the death overs – Porel smashed 3 fours and 3 sixes in a blistering cameo. LSG finished at a mammoth **219/4**. SRH's bowlers – Zampa, Ellis, Patil – all went past 7 economy, with Tripuresh Singh bleeding 63 in 4 overs.

Chasing 220, SRH lost **Jos Buttler** (6) early to **Arshdeep Singh**. Then came a masterclass – **P Simran Singh** (74 off 44) and **KL Rahul** (67 off 39) added 143 runs for the second wicket. The required rate hovered around 10, but the pair kept it within reach. Then the collapse. Rahul fell in the 15th over. Arshdeep returned to remove Simran Singh in the 17th. **Ashwin Das** (3/42) and Arshdeep (4/41) choked the life out of the chase. SRH went from 150/1 to **193/8** in 20 overs – falling 26 runs short. LSG defended a massive total with clinical death bowling. Arshdeep's 4-wicket haul sealed a well-deserved Player of the Match award.
