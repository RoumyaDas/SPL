# 🏏 Match 49 – KKR vs RR: Kishan's Masterclass Steals the Show! 🌟

RR won the toss and elected to bat on a pitch favoring pacers. They lost an early wicket but recovered well through a blazing 55 from Kieron Pollard and a steady 34 from Shivam Dube. However, a brilliant bowling display from Bhuvneshwar Kumar (3 wickets) and Spencer Johnson (2 wickets) restricted RR to a modest total of 188. The innings lost momentum in the death overs, managing only a few boundaries in the final stages.

Chasing 189, KKR lost an early wicket but found their anchor in Ishan Kishan. He played a sensational, unbeaten century, anchoring the chase with calm precision. Cameron Green provided the perfect support with a quickfire 48*, as the duo stitched a match-winning partnership. They chased down the target with 14 balls to spare, completing a clinical victory. It was a masterclass in chase management from Kishan!
