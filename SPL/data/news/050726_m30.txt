**Match 30 – CSK vs MI**: A Heart-Stopping Finale at the CSK Fortress! 🏟️

On a pace-friendly track, CSK's decision to bat first after losing the toss proved to be a masterstroke. Tilak Varma was in sublime touch, anchoring the innings with a magnificent 91 off 46, while T Stubbs provided the late fireworks with a rapid 50 off 25. Together, they powered CSK to a formidable 218.

The chase was a rollercoaster of emotions. Urvil Patel led MI's charge with a spectacular 97 off 52, while Abhishek Sharma chipped in with a gritty 46, keeping the required rate in check. With MI needing just 15 runs off the last two overs, the game hung in the balance. However, Geet Puri bowled a sensational 19th over, conceding just 2 runs and picking up two crucial wickets. The equation came down to 4 runs off the final over, but Puri held his nerve, bowling a maiden over with a runout on the last ball, sealing a dramatic 2-run victory for CSK. It was a finish that had everyone on the edge of their seats!
