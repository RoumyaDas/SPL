# 🏏 SPL S08 - Match 25: SRH vs DC

## A One-Man Show and a Clinical Chase! 💥

DC's bowlers set the tone early, with **JJ Bumrah** (4/22) and **Shaheen Shah Afridi** (4/37) running through the SRH batting lineup like a hot knife through butter. Despite a fighting half-century from **P Simran Singh** (56 off 33), SRH crumbled to 161 all out in just 18 overs. 

In response, **GJ Maxwell** (96* off 54) played a masterclass, anchoring the chase with a flawless blend of power and finesse. He found able support from **N Sindhu** (33 off 19), as DC chased down the target with 17 balls to spare, sealing a comprehensive victory.

It was a clinical performance from DC, led by their star all-rounder's brilliance. 🌟
