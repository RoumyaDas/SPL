# 🏏 SPL S07 Match 20: RCB vs RR – The Maharaj Spell & de Silva's Lone War

RR won the toss and sent RCB in on a spin-friendly pitch (4x spin assist). RCB's top order crumbled early – **Gayle** (10), **Pathak** (33), **Patidar** (1), **Sharma** (4) – all fell cheaply. At 71/7 in the 9th over, RCB were staring at a disaster. Then came **Wanindu Hasaranga de Silva** (80 off 36) – a one-man army. He smashed 6 fours and 6 sixes, dragging RCB to a fighting total. **Keshav Maharaj** (25* off 32) held one end up as de Silva went berserk in the death overs – 20 runs off the 18th, 19 off the 19th. RCB finished at **178/7**. Ghazanfar (2/28) and Karan Lal (2/34) were economical.

Chasing 179, RR lost **Kohli** (1) early. **Jaiswal** (16) and **Salt** (20) fell soon after. Enter **Glenn Phillips** (59 off 29) – he smashed 3 fours and 5 sixes in a breathtaking counterattack. But **Keshav Maharaj** had other plans. The left-arm spinner ran through RR's middle and lower order – **Dube** (15), **Aniket Verma** (39), **Anmolpreet** (6), **Karan Lal** (5) – all fell to him. Maharaj finished with stunning figures of **5/19** in 4 overs. RR collapsed from 113/4 to **168 all out** in 19.4 overs – falling 10 runs short. RCB defended a low total on a spin-friendly track. Maharaj's fifer was a masterclass in pressure bowling. Player of the Match – Keshav Maharaj.
