# 🏏 SPL S07 Match 12: SRH vs DC – Ellis Storms in the Death

SRH batted first on a balanced pitch. Openers Short (38) and Simran Singh (18) started briskly, but KL Rahul (65 off 34, 4 fours, 4 sixes) and Kartik Sharma (28 off 12) exploded — taking 28 runs off one AK Markram over. VR Iyer (42) and Hardik Pandya (23* off 8) pushed SRH to 234/7. Bumrah's 3/46 for DC was a rare bright spot.

Chasing 235, DC lost Maxwell early, but Tarang Gohel (46) and Suryakumar Yadav (80 off 41, 7 fours, 4 sixes) kept the chase alive. SKY's stunning knock ended via a bizarre hit-wicket in the 16th over. N Sindhu (51) fought on, but SRH needed a hero. Enter NT Ellis — he returned in the death to choke DC, conceding just 14 runs in his last two overs (18th & 20th) and picking 3/56 overall. DC fell 17 runs short in a nerve-shredding finish.

**Winner:** SRH  
**Player of the Match:** SA Yadav (DC)
