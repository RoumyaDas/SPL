📝 **SPL S07 Match 21: DC vs RCB – A Run-Fest for the Ages**

The DC fortress lived up to its batting-friendly reputation as two modern-day giants collided in a six-hitting symphony. RCB won the toss and opted to bowl first, a decision that quickly backfired when Tarang Gohel departed for a golden duck off Chirag Jani. But what followed was absolute carnage.

Enter **GJ Maxwell**, who played one of the most destructive T20 knocks you'll ever witness. Unbeaten on 143 off just 64 balls, the big man plundered 14 fours and 9 sixes, treating every RCB bowler with equal disdain. He found able support from N Sindhu, who smashed a blistering 73 off 35. Together, they added 157 runs for the third wicket, propelling DC to a monstrous 243/7. HJ Manenti (3/46) was the only bowler with any solace.

Chasing 244, RCB needed a miracle – and **CH Gayle** nearly delivered one. The Universe Boss remained unbeaten on 118 off 62, launching 10 fours and 6 sixes in a captain's knock. Abhishek Pathak (55 off 30) provided fiery support, and cameos from Priyansh Arya (39) and Salil Arora (19) kept the chase alive. But the asking rate kept climbing. Despite Gayle's heroics, RCB fell just 5 runs short, finishing at 238/3. JJ Bumrah's miserly 1/24 in 4 overs proved the difference.

A heart-stopping thriller where batting brilliance met just enough bowling steel. Maxwell walks away with the POTM, but Gayle's effort will be remembered too. DC win by 5 runs. 🏏🔥
