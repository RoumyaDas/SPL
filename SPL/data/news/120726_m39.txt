# 🏏 SPL S08 – Match 39: GT vs LSG – A Run-Fest and a Clinical Chase

GT won the toss and elected to bat first on a balanced pitch, and their openers set the tone immediately. JP Inglis was in devastating form, smashing 82 off just 37 balls, while Naman Dhir played a perfect supporting role with an unbeaten 67. The duo put on a massive partnership, and late fireworks from R Parag (40) and V Nigam (13* off 4) propelled GT to a mammoth 220/4 in their 20 overs. LSG's bowlers had no answers to the onslaught, with only Kuldeep Yadav managing to pick up two wickets.

Chasing 221, LSG's innings was in tatters right from the start. They lost wickets in clusters, with Ashutosh Sharma and GF Linde running through the top order. Abishek Porel (43) and Sameer Rizvi (44) tried to rebuild, but the required rate kept climbing. HH Pandya played a valiant knock of 54 off 27 balls, but it was too little too late as LSG kept losing wickets at regular intervals. They were eventually bowled out for 182 in 18.2 overs, handing GT a comprehensive victory. A clinical all-round performance from GT, with Inglis's brilliance with the bat and a disciplined bowling effort sealing the win.
