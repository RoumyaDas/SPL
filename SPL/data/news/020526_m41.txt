🏏 **SPL S07, Match 41: MI vs RCB – Arya and de Silva Power RCB to a High-Scoring Win**

MI won the toss and elected to bowl first on a spin-friendly pitch. RCB's openers Atharva Taide (21 off 18) and Priyansh Arya (83 off 36) added 62 runs before Taide fell. Arya then combined with Wanindu Hasaranga de Silva (57* off 37) to keep the scoreboard racing. Salil Arora (34 off 19) provided late fireworks as RCB posted a massive 220/5. Shivam Shukla was MI's best bowler with 3/38.

In reply, MI openers Tushar Raheja (40 off 25) and Urvil Patel (62 off 39) started steadily, adding 78 runs. After Raheja's dismissal, Abhishek Sharma joined the party, smashing 63 off 31 (6 fours, 3 sixes). But wickets kept falling at crucial moments – Keshav Maharaj (3/40) and Abrar Ahmed (2/55) choked MI in the middle overs. Despite Nicholas Pooran's late 29 off 8 (4 fours, 2 sixes), MI fell agonizingly short by 4 runs. RCB's spin attack held their nerve in a thrilling finish.

**Winner:** RCB  
**Player of the Match:** Priyansh Arya (RCB) – for his breathtaking 83 off 36 that set up the match.
